1. Introduction


Overview of the E-commerce Portal Module
The E-commerce Portal Module enables retailers to extend their physical store operations to an online
platform, allowing customers to browse products, place orders, and complete transactions digitally. This
module integrates seamlessly with the POS system, ensuring real-time inventory updates, order
management, and smooth transaction processing.



Benefits of Integrating E-commerce with POS
   •   Expanded Customer Reach: Allows retailers to sell beyond their physical location and reach a
       broader audience.

   •   Real-Time Inventory Sync: Ensures accurate stock levels between the online store and POS
       system, preventing overselling or stockouts.

   •   Streamlined Order Management: Orders placed online are automatically reflected in the POS
       system, simplifying processing and fulfillment.

   •   Enhanced Customer Convenience: Customers can shop anytime, increasing sales potential and
       customer satisfaction.

   •   Automated Reporting & Insights: Provides detailed reports on online sales, customer behavior,
       and inventory movement to help businesses make data-driven decisions.

   •   Flexible Payment Options: Supports multiple payment methods, enhancing the overall shopping
       experience for customers.

2. Enabling the E-commerce Module
Prerequisites
Here we require Back-Office access of RapidRMS: RapidRMS Backoffice Login




Step-by-step activation process in POS
   1. Login to "BOS" with your user credentials.

   2. Tap "MENU" (located on the top left side).

   3. Go into "SETTING".

   4. In "SETTING", go into "MODULE".

   5. Activate the “E-COMMERCE” module.

3. Domain Configuration
How to obtain a domain for the E-commerce portal
    1. Login to "BOS" with your user credentials.

    2. Tap "MENU" (located on the top left side).

    3. Go into "PROFILE".

    4. In "PROFILE", go into "COMPANY".

    5. Go into "STORE INFO".

    6. Here, you will find your "E-COMMERCE DOMAIN" details.




Linking the domain to the store
❖ Please use the following link to link the domain to your store:

    http://YourDomainName.dinenshop.com/home/shop



❖ Here, you need to replace "YourDomainName" with the domain details received from the "BACK-
  OFFICE".

    For example, if your domain details are "BhaveshPatel", then your e-commerce portal link will be:

    http://BhaveshPatel.dinenshop.com/home/shop

4. E-commerce Portal Settings
Accessing E-commerce Settings
To manage e-commerce settings, follow these steps:

   1. Login to "BOS" with your user credentials.

   2. Tap "MENU" (located on the top left side).

   3. Go into "E-COMMERCE".

   4. In "E-COMMERCE", go into "E-COMMERCE SETTING".




General Settings and Customization Options
   •   "Store Hours": Enable or disable e-commerce operations for your store.

   •   "Hours Schedule": Choose between a fixed or customized schedule for e-commerce operations.

   •   "Order Ready Time": Set the estimated time (in minutes) required to prepare an order.

   •   "User Set Pickup Time": Allow customers to choose their preferred pickup time.



Pricing and Markup Settings
   •   "E-commerce Markup Price": Define additional pricing for online orders, either as a fixed amount
       ($) or a percentage (%) increase.

Delivery and Pickup Options


   •   "Delivery Option": Enable or disable various fulfillment methods, such as:

           o   In-store Pickup

           o   Curbside Pickup

           o   Shipping




Payment Options


   •   Pay At Store – Allows customers to place an order online and pay in-store at the time of pickup.

   •   Pay with Credit Card – Enables credit card payment processing through the selected payment
       gateway.

   •   Pay with PayPal – Allows customers to make payments via PayPal.

   •   Pay At Pump Gateway – Allows configuring the payment processor for fuel-related transactions
       at the pump.

   •   ECommerce Gateway – Defines the payment processor used for online transactions within the E-
       commerce portal.



Pages (Selectable Content for E-commerce Site)
   •   About Us – Displays a page with business information and details.

   •   Event – Enables an events page to showcase promotions, discounts, or special occasions.

   •   Blog – Allows adding a blog section to the website for updates and news.

   •   Contact Us – Provides a contact page for customer inquiries.



Additional Settings (Toggle Options)
   •   Food Menu – Enables or disables the display of a food menu on the E-commerce portal.

   •   Pay At Pump – Allows customers to make fuel purchases directly through the E-commerce portal.

   •   Age Verification – Enables age verification for restricted items like alcohol or tobacco.

   3. Notifications can be configured to alert staff via:

           o   POS screen notifications

           o   Email notifications to specified recipients
