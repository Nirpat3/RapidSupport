        iPad Stand                   When purchasing an iPad stand, be sure to check both the iPad size and cable management features. A stand that supports multiple iPad sizes is recommended. If you're buying a dual-sided iPad stand, confirm the screen size compatibility to ensure the stand works with your device.                                                                                                                                             
        Bluetooth Barcode Scanner    iOS tablets support Bluetooth-based barcode scanners, and any scanner compatible with iOS will work. If you are purchasing a USB-based barcode scanner, ensure it is iOS-compatible. You will also need an additional USB dongle to provide both power and connectivity. All scanners connect to the iPad as a keyboard input device.                                                                                               
        Networking                   To ensure secure and uninterrupted connectivity for your POS and payment equipment, we strongly recommend setting up a dedicated network exclusively for the POS ecosystem. Avoid sharing this network with other connections, such as employee Wi-Fi. For assistance with setting up a dedicated network, please contact Rapid.                                                                                                    
        Pay-At-Pump                  Rapid supports Pay-at-Pump only through Verifone Commander. If your pumps are compatible with Verifone Commander, they can be enabled for Pay-at-Pump. To make this work, your pumps must have EMV-compliant equipment, proper EMV wiring or wireless connection from pump to the store, and a Mako like Firewall to ensure secure payment transactions.
