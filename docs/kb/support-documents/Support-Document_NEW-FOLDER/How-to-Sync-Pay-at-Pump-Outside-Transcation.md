# How to Sync Pay-at-Pump Outside Transcation

**[How to Sync Pay-at-Pump Outside Transcation?]{.underline}**

**Plese do this process to uploading your Pay-at-Pump invoices in RapidRMS**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to RapidRMS Application with your credentials.

- Enter your Email OR User ID.

- Enter your Password.

- Click on SIGN IN button.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="3.008707349081365in"}

2.  Click on "**CASH REGISTER**".

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="2.864634733158355in"}

3.  Ener your quick password and Click on "**ENTER**" button.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="3.312424540682415in"}

4.  Tap menu ![hamburger-menu-icon-png-white-24.jpg](media/image4.png){width="0.2075404636920385in" height="0.2075404636920385in"} (located on the top left side corner)

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="4.08657261592301in"}

5.  In Menu, click on the "**SYNC OUTSIDE TRANSCATION**".

![](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="3.960508530183727in"}

6.  Please wait for process. It will take sometime depends on your number of invoices.

![](media/image7.png){width="6.5in" height="3.7694925634295715in"}

![](media/image8.png){width="6.5in" height="3.7897845581802274in"}

7.  At the end of process, you will get popup message that "**Invoice are successfully uploaded**".

![](media/image9.png){width="6.5in" height="4.066720253718286in"}
