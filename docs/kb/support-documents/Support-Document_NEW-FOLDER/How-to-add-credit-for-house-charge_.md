# How to add credit for house charge_

**[How to Add Credit for House Charge?]{.underline}**

When a customer wants to add some credit for house charge payment in the store then it is possible to add credit.

For add credit limit follow below step.

- Open rapid RMS application.

- Go to the customer loyalty module.

- From customer list, view that customer.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.722222222222222in"}

- From profile, tap to house charge tab.

- At the bottom bar, one option is available as add credit hit that button.

- Enter credit amount you want to give to house charge.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.805555555555555in"}

- From RCR that payment will complete.

- At the time of add credit you can pay by any other payment rather than house charge.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.75in"}

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.736111111111111in"}

- Now in house charge page added credit would display.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="4.708333333333333in"}

- If there is already negative amount is available then when adding credit then that will deduct and remaining amount display in balance amount.
