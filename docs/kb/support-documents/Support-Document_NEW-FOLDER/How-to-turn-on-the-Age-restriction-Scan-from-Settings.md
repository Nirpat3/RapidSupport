# How to turn on the Age restriction Scan from Settings

**[How to turn on the Age restriction Scan from Settings?]{.underline}**

1.  Go to DashBoard.

2.  Click on the settings option ![C:\\Users\\mit shah\\AppData\\Local\\Microsoft\\Windows\\INetCache\\IE\\BU8Y79T3\\WMF-Agora-Settings_BCBCBC.svg\[1\].png](media/image1.png){width="0.17463910761154855in" height="0.17463910761154855in"} available at the bottom of the DashBoard.

![C:\\Users\\mit shah\\Downloads\\MicrosoftTeams-image (8).png](media/image2.jpeg){width="6.268055555555556in" height="3.65541447944007in"}

3.  Enter your quick access password.

4.  Under Rapid Settings, click on options.

5.  Turn on the toggle button for the Driver License Scan option.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.268055555555556in" height="3.6770833333333335in"}

6.  Now, go to the Cash register module and ring up the item is associated with Age verification.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.268055555555556in" height="4.326435914260718in"}

7.  Click on Driver's License option beside the DOB Tab.

8.  Scan the QR barcode of the document.

9.  Item will ring up, after scanning the identity document of the customers.
