# How can we activate GIFT CARD Service from POS

**[How can we activate GIFT CARD Service from POS ?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to **RapidRMS Application** with your credentials.

Enter your Email OR User ID.

Enter your Password.

Click on SIGN IN button.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="3.008707349081365in"}

2.  Click on "**INVENTORY MANAGEMENT**".

Ener your quick password and Click on "**ENTER**" button.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="3.3301695100612423in"}

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="3.760482283464567in"}

3.  Tap menu ![hamburger-menu-icon-png-white-24.jpg](media/image4.png){width="0.2075404636920385in" height="0.2075404636920385in"} (located on the top left side corner) & Click on "**DEPARTMENT**" option.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="3.6404669728783903in"}

4.  In Department, You will find "**SERVICES**" option, Click on it.

In Services, You can please toggle ON for "**Gift Card**".

![](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="3.560456036745407in"}
