# How to add department subdepartment in item

**[How to add a department/sub-department to an item?]{.underline}**

If you want to categorize your items in department/sub-department wise then add department/sub-department in your Item.

Here are the steps to manage your Department/sub-department in an item.

- Open the RapidRMS application on your iPad.

- Select Inventory Management from the dashboard.

- Enter your quick access password.

- Click on add button from the bottom of the screen to create a new item or click on any item from the list to update an existing Item.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.736111111111111in"}

- Add/Edit Item screen will open.

- At the top of the screen, there are Info, Pricing, Discount, History, and Options tabs available.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.777777777777778in"}

- The Department section is available under the Info tab.

- It resides unassign department/subdepartment in the Department section.

- Click on Department / Sub-Department option.

- The list of departments / Sub-departments will appear. You can select any department/sub-department from the list.

- Department will be updated on the item.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.666666666666667in"}

- If you want to create a new department and add a new department/subdepartment then click on the "Add New Department/subdepartment" button from the bottom of the page.

- Fill necessary detail and click on the Save button.

- A newly added department/subdepartment will appear on the list.

- Select department/subdepartment from list.

- Click on the Save button.

- Please note that you can select only one department/sub-department in any item.

- Department/subdepartment will be added to an item.

- Click on the **Save** button to apply changes in Item.
