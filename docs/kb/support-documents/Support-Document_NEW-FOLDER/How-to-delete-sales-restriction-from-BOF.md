# How to delete sales restriction from BOF

**How to delete sales restriction from BOF ?**

When whole entry of sales restriction need to delete then all items under that sales restriction will remove from that restriction list.

For delete sales restriction follow below procedure.

- Log in with bof office web url.

- Make sure e commerce option should ON in store.

- Go to blue law tab.

- Click on action button.

- Hit delete button.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="3.527837926509186in"}

- If in sales restriction items are available then that entry would not allow to delete.

- It display validation.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="3.361111111111111in"}

- When in sales restriction no items are there then that entry can be deleted.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="3.637688101487314in"}

- Give yes.

- Entry will remove from list.
