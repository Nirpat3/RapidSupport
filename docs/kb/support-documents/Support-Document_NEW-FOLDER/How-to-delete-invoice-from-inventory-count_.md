# How to delete invoice from inventory count_

**How to delete invoice from inventory count?**

- In inventory count, counting invoice is displayed in the recall, join, reconcile and in the history page.

- When an invoice is in recall, join count and in reconciliation at that time you can delete the invoice by swiping that invoice.

- In history, invoice can not be deleted. Only you can view.
