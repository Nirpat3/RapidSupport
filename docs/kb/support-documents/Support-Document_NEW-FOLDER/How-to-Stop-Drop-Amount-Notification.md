# How to Stop Drop Amount Notification

**[How to Stop Drop Amount Notification?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to BackOffice(BOS) with your user credentials.

2.  Tap menu ![](media/image1.png){width="0.20499890638670165in" height="0.20499890638670165in"} (located on the top left side).

3.  Tap **SETTINGS**.

4.  Tap **STORE**.

5.  Scroll to see the "**PAYMENT TYPE**" option. You will find your existing list of Tender.

> ![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="2.6682950568678914in"}

6.  Click on "**ACTION**" then click on "**EDIT"** to check the status of Drop Amount Notification.

> ![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="2.7858705161854767in"}

7.  Please uncheck "**Drop Amount**" option, it will stop "Drop Amount Notification".

> ![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="2.864283683289589in"}
