# How can I see my purchase order history

**[How can I see my purchase order history?]{.underline}**

- When you reconcile any purchase order, your order will be moved to history section in Purchase order. You can find, print and email the purchase order history from History section.

<!-- -->

- Your history is listed as individual records, starting with the most recent.

<!-- -->

- Open Rapid RMS application.

- Select Purchase order module from the dashboard.

> ![C:\\Users\\mit shah\\Downloads\\MicrosoftTeams-image (8).png](media/image1.jpeg){width="6.268055555555556in" height="3.65541447944007in"}

- Enter your quick access password.

- Select Order History option.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.228261154855643in"}

- It will appear the purchase order list which is completed.

- It will display all the details of the purchase order like create Date, OPN#, title, Remarks, Invoice #, Delivery date & time and user name.

![C:\\Users\\siya\\Downloads\\MicrosoftTeams-image (80).png](media/image3.png){width="6.494445538057743in" height="3.9456528871391074in"}

- Click on any records from the list.

- It will display all the item details which were reconciled for the selected order.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.576783683289589in"}

- It will appear calculation details of items, received qty, and cost of an item.

- If you want to print or email the purchase order.

- At bottom, click on export button.

- Select Print option.

- It will appear a purchase order history.

- At top right side, click on arrow button.

- Select print option to print a purchase order history report.

- Select Email option to send email.

- Enter email address that you want to send email.

- Click on Send button. The purchase order details will be sent over email.

- And from export option print and email can be done.
