# How to refund in house charge_

**How to refund in house charge?**

In house charge when a customer wants to refund amount then it is possible in customer loyalty.

- Open rapid RMS application.

- Go to the cash register.

- From RCR go to customer loyalty and select any customer.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.805555555555555in"}

- View that customer and go to house charge.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.777777777777778in"}

- Enter amount.

- At the bottom bar, there is one option refund.

- Tap on refund option.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.680555555555555in"}

- Complete transaction by any other payment type then house charge i.e cash.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.708333333333333in"}
