# How to turn on the DOB required for an age verification option from settings

**[How to turn on the DOB required for an age verification option from settings?]{.underline}**

- Due to the different ways that a responsible adult can order, or be served, alcohol there are also different methods to ensure the patron of an establishment or online supplier can legally receive alcohol.

- When you ring up any item which is associated with an age-restricted department, the Age verification window will appear as shown in the below image.

<!-- -->

- There are three options available to enter Age verification details.

1)  The Cashier can enter the date of birth of the customer manually. It will not allow to ring up the item until cashier do not enter the date of birth.

> ![C:\\Users\\mit shah\\Documents\\Lightshot\\Screenshot_27.png](media/image1.png){width="6.268211942257218in" height="3.4096347331583554in"}

2)  The Cashier can select the options of the asked question for Age verification criteria available below at the window.

> **For example:-** If any customer visit your store frequently or you know that customer is adult, it does not require to enter date of birth or scan their document. You can simply select **Yes** option of asked question from the bottom.
>
> ![C:\\Users\\mit shah\\Downloads\\MicrosoftTeams-image (13).png](media/image2.jpeg){width="6.262498906386702in" height="3.3913046806649167in"}

3)  The Cashier can select the option to scan the identity document to verify the age of the customer.

> ![](media/image3.png){width="6.268055555555556in" height="3.6488013998250217in"}

- Although the Cashier wants to turn on the DOB required for an age verification option, the cashier can only enter to ensure the date of birth of the customer.

1.  Click on the settings option ![C:\\Users\\mit shah\\AppData\\Local\\Microsoft\\Windows\\INetCache\\IE\\BU8Y79T3\\WMF-Agora-Settings_BCBCBC.svg\[1\].png](media/image4.png){width="0.17463910761154855in" height="0.17463910761154855in"} available at the bottom of the DashBoard.

> ![C:\\Users\\mit shah\\Downloads\\MicrosoftTeams-image (8).png](media/image5.jpeg){width="6.268055555555556in" height="3.9948458005249345in"}

2.  Enter your quick access password.

3.  Under Rapid Settings, click on options.

4.  Turn on the toggle button for DOB required for age verification.

> ![C:\\Users\\mit shah\\Documents\\Lightshot\\Screenshot_25.png](media/image6.png){width="6.272042869641295in" height="3.4782600612423447in"}

- As you can see in the below image, It will only allow to ring up an item after the cashier enters the Date of birth of the customer.
