# How to do payment in RCR

**How to do the payment in RCR?**

When selling/returning any item then need to do the payment. Rapid RMS applications give the facility to pay the amount by various payment types.

For payment follow the below procedure.

- Go to the cash register.

- Ring up items and click on item total.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.791666666666667in"}

- It redirects to the tender screen.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.763888888888889in"}

- There are payment types available like cash, check, credit, EBT, Gift card, House Charge, or any other which are set by the payment master.

- In the tender screen, payment is possible from more than one payment type.

- In credit cards split payments can be done.

- In the side panel, there is Numpad from there you can set the amount in any payment type manually.

- At the bottom bar, there is a fix option available that can be used directly and the change amount will return.

- After the set, the payment method clicks on Done.

- The transaction will be complete.
