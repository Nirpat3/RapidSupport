# How to check Drop Amount Report in BackOffice

[How to check Drop Amount Reports in BackOffice ?]{.underline}

1.  Login on the Back-Office website.

2.  Left side, you can see an option with "ANALYTICS", click on it.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.267716535433071in" height="1.1944444444444444in"}

3.  In Analytics, You will find the "REPORT" option, click on it.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.267716535433071in" height="2.0694444444444446in"}

4.  In Report, you will find the "STAFF" option, click on it.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.267716535433071in" height="2.875in"}

5.  In Staff, you will find the "DROP AMOUNT" option, here you can select date range as per your required period and you can see your Drop Amount Report.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.267716535433071in" height="3.0in"}
