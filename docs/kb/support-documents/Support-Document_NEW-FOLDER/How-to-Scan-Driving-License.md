# How to Scan Driving License

**[How to Scan Driving License?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to RapidRMS Application with your credentials.

- Enter your Email OR User ID.

- Enter your Password.

- Click on SIGN IN button.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="2.744938757655293in"}

2.  Click on "**SETTING**".

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="3.24876312335958in"}

3.  Please enable "**DRIVER LICENSE SCAN**" button.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="3.714601924759405in"}

4.  Now, whenever if any item ringup with Age Resrictin Pop-up then you can click on "**Driving License**" option as per below image.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="3.75328302712161in"}
