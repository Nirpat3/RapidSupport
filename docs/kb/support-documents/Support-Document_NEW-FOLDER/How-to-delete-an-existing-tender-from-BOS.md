# How to delete an existing tender from BOS

**[How to delete an existing tender from BOS?]{.underline}**

- Log in to the Back office with your credentials.

- Now go to settings. You may need to scroll down to select the settings option.

- Select the Store option from the list.

- Click on the payment type option from the list.

<!-- -->

- Click on Action button next to payment.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="3.008986220472441in"}

- Click or tap on the Delete button you wish to delete the tender.

- The tender will be deleted and it will not appear in the list.
