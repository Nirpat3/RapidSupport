# How to Load Balance in Gift Card

**[How to Load Balance in a Gift Card?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Open Rapid RMS application.

2.  Go to the Cash Register.

3.  At the bottom bar, there is one option named as "**MORE**" click on that option.

> Click on a "**GIFTCARD**" from all of them.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.131620734908137in"}

4.  When hit gift card then there are two options available. Please click on "**Load Balance**".

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="3.712475940507437in"}

5.  Enter the gift card number and confirm it again & Add the amount in \$ of which amount you want to balance for gift card. Click on "**SAVE**".

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.130208880139983in"}

6.  A message will prompt "Would you like to add \$100.00 to Card#00069600000074 ?", Click on YES.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.535212160979878in"}

7.  Once you click on "**YES**", it will redirect to ringup screen.

You can finish your transcation with any payment option like (**Cash, Debit, Credit**) except RapidGiftCard as you are creating new gift card.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9453215223097113in"}

![](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="4.503953412073491in"}
