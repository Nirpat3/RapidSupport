# How to edit category of the department from Back office

**[How to edit the category of the department from Back-office?]{.underline}**

- Please note that when you change the category name, its effect will appear in Department, Shirt report, X Report, Z Report, and ZZ report as well.

<!-- -->

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to BOS with your user credentials.

2.  Tap menu ![hamburger-menu-icon-png-white-24.jpg](media/image1.png){width="0.2075404636920385in" height="0.2075404636920385in"} (located on the top right side)

3.  Tap **Inventory \> Department Category** option.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9762259405074367in"}

4.  Click on the **action** button.

5.  To edit the category, click on the **Edit** button.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="3.0354199475065617in"}

6.  Now, update or change the category name.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9689588801399824in"}

7.  Tap on the Save button.
