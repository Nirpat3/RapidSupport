# How do I download customer display and activate store

**How do I download customer display and activate store?**

- First, you need to go to the App Store on the iPad.

- Then, download customer display from the App Store.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.791666666666667in"}

- After you downloaded customer display, you will have to click on the customer display App.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.722222222222222in"}

- It will ask you to login Id and password. You need to enter back-office id and password which one you use for your store.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.166666666666667in"}

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.791666666666667in"}

- Then, it will ask you which POS you want to activate for customer display. Click on POS which one you want to activate.

- Then, you need to go dashboard setting at the bottom on the screen in the POS iPad.

- On the right, click on the customer display. Then, click on search but make sure in both iPad Wi-Fi must be same otherwise it won't connect.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="4.666666666666667in"}
