# How to change GROUP PRICE

**[How to change GROUP PRICE ?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to BackOffice with your credentials.

2.  Tap menu ![hamburger-menu-icon-png-white-24.jpg](media/image1.png){width="0.2075404636920385in" height="0.2075404636920385in"} (located on the top left side)

3.  Click on "**INVENTORY**" Section.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="2.484831583552056in"}

4.  In INVENTORY, click on "**GROUP PRICE CHANGE**" option.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="3.01288823272091in"}

5.  In Group Price Change, you will find 2 options {LIST & ADD}

- **LIST** : you can see your updated price change list.

- **ADD** : you can change your group price from here.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="2.3042836832895888in"}

6.  Go into "**ADD**" option.

- Here we have given 4 categories to change your price. You can change price through any categories.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="2.8925492125984253in"}

(For Example : Here we have selected "Department" category to change our price, and we have to select particular department from Department list whichever department items price we have to change.)

![](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="3.177490157480315in"}

7.  Here, we have selected "TANZANIA" Department and all item will be display from "TANZANIA" Department.

You can select your item whichever item price you want to change OR you can select ALL option also if you want to change price for all items in that Department.

Click on "NEXT" button.

![](media/image7.png){width="6.5in" height="3.31129593175853in"}

8.  Once you click on "**NEXT**" button then you will find 2-options in 2^nd^ EDIT step {**CUSTOME PRICE** & **VARIABLE PRICE**\].

![](media/image8.png){width="6.5in" height="3.2482195975503063in"}

9.  In CUSTOME PRICE, you can simply entre your price (whichever price you want to update) and click on "NEXT" button.

![](media/image9.png){width="6.5in" height="3.1641666666666666in"}

10. In 3^rd^ Overview step, you can review as well your new price then click on "FINISH" button.

![](media/image10.png){width="6.5in" height="3.278195538057743in"}

11. In Variable Price, You can change your price by Increase -- Decreasing option by \$ OR % from your existing price.

![](media/image11.png){width="6.5in" height="3.265972222222222in"}

12. Suppose if you want to increase price on your existing price then go with "INCREASE" option and if you want to descrease price on your existing price then go with "DECREASE" option.

13. For Example, If we have to increase 10 % then we can select "INCREASE" and \"%" option with entering 10 in box like below image & click on "SAVE" button.

![](media/image12.png){width="6.5in" height="3.2665299650043744in"}

14. Once you click on "SAVE" button then you have to just click on box whichever price you want to increase then it will catch 10 in that box. And after that click on "NEXT" button.

![](media/image13.png){width="6.5in" height="3.215463692038495in"}

15. In 3^rd^ Overview step, you can review as well your new price then click on "FINISH" button.
