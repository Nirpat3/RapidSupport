# Update third party promotions RJR

**[How to update third-party promotions for RJR scan Data?]{.underline}**

1.  Login to BOS with your user credentials.

2.  At the top left side, click on the three-line hamburger ![hamburger-menu-icon-png-white-24.jpg](media/image1.png){width="0.2075404636920385in" height="0.2075404636920385in"} menu.

3.  Choose a Third-party promotion option from the settings.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="3.0108377077865267in"}

4.  Select the existing third party discount from the list.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9998775153105863in"}

5.  Click on ![C:\\Users\\siya\\AppData\\Local\\Microsoft\\Windows\\INetCache\\IE\\EHUWMOOS\\plus_PNG121\[1\].png](media/image4.png){width="0.21738517060367454in" height="0.21738517060367454in"} plus button is located beside the Item name.

6.  Click on the action button and select the edit option from the drop-down list.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="2.994536307961505in"}

7.  Update details.

8.  At the top left side, click on the Finish button.

![](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="3.0113648293963253in"}
