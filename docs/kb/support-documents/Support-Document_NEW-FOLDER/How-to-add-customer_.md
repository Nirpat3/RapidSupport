# How to add customer_

**How to add a customer?**

When a customer is added in the bill then all transactions of that customer include in customer loyalty.

For connecting customer, there are various different way as per the following.

1.  **Add customer from Bill.**

- Open rapid RMS application.

- Go to the cash register module.

- Click on Customer add option which is available at the top on the bill.

- When clicking on that option then customer list will open select any customer.

- Now complete transaction. Invoice Detail will display in customer loyalty.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.276042213473316in"}

2.  **Add customer from the tender screen[.]{.underline}**

- Open rapid RMS application.

- Go to the cash register module.

- In bill ring up items and go to the tender screen.

- Click on Customer add option which is available at the top left corner.

- When clicking on that option then customer list will open select any customer.

- Now complete transaction. Invoice Detail will display in customer loyalty.

**[E g;]{.underline}**![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.251847112860893in"}

3.  **Add customer from the invoice screen.**

- Open rapid RMS application.

- Go to the cash register module.

- Go to invoice screen.

- Click on Customer add option which is available at the top left corner.

- When clicking on that option then customer list will open select any customer.

- Particular Invoice Detail will display in customer loyalty.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.934646762904637in"}
