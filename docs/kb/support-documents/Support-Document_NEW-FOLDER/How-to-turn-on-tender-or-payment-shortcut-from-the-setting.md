# How to turn on tender or payment shortcut from the setting

**[How to turn on tender or payment shortcut from the setting?]{.underline}**

- **[Please follow the below steps:-]{.underline}**

<!-- -->

- Open rapid RMS application.

- At the bottom, click on the setting option.

- Enter a quick access password.

![C:\\Users\\mit shah\\Downloads\\MicrosoftTeams-image (8).png](media/image1.jpeg){width="6.268055555555556in" height="3.65541447944007in"}

- Select the Tender configuration option from the right side option.

- It will appear the list of all available tender.

- Select tender from the list.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.8684208223972005in"}

- There will appear the Tender Shortcut toggle button.

- By default, the Tender Shortcut toggle button is turned off.

- Turn on the Tender Shortcut toggle button.

![MicrosoftTeams-image (92).png](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.875in"}

- When you turn on the Tender Shortcut toggle button, the Tender shortcut will appear at the bottom left side corner of the Cash register screen.

- As you can see in the below image, The check tender appears at the bottom left side corner of the Cash register screen.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.788920603674541in"}
