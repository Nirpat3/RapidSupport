# How to Export Inventory for DOORDASH_MANUAL Process_NEW

**[How to Export Inventory for DOORDASH E-Commerce ? (MANUAL PROCESS)]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to BOS with your user credentials.

2.  Tap menu ![hamburger-menu-icon-png-white-24.jpg](media/image1.png){width="0.2075404636920385in" height="0.2075404636920385in"} (located on the top left side).

3.  Click on "**E-COMMERCE VENDOR**" Section.

4.  In E-Commerce Vendor, click on "**CONFIGURATION**".

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="2.8668307086614173in"}

5.  In Configuration, click on "**ADD**" Section.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="1.068888888888889in"}

6.  We have 2-sections to creat E-Commerce Vendor (**INFO** & **ITEMS**).

First, Please fillup the "**INFO**" details.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="3.2004101049868767in"}

+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **File Configuration**                                                                                                                             |
+===================================+:===============================================================================================================+
| **Status**                        | We need to "ON" toggle button to active status.                                                                |
+-----------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **File Upload**                   | You can select File Upload Process "Manually".                                                                 |
+-----------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Type**                          | You can select either \$ **OR** %.                                                                             |
+-----------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Price Plus**                    | You can put your additional Price whatever price you want to add on your existing price for E-Commerce Vendor. |
+-----------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Store Info**                                                                                                                                     |
+-----------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **E-Commerce Vendor**             | Put your E-Commerce Vendor Name                                                                                |
+-----------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Customer No.**                  | This details, we will get it from E-Commerce Vendor.                                                           |
+-----------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **StoreName**                     | This details automatically catch base on your BackOffice.                                                      |
+-----------------------------------+                                                                                                                |
| **Store ID**                      |                                                                                                                |
+-----------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Save**                          | We will click on "SAVE" button after adding our items list.                                                    |
+-----------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

7.  Please select column as per your Vendor File requirement.

[For Example]{.underline}, Here DOORDASH asked for 3 details (UPC, Item Name-Description and Price), So we have selected that 3 column only.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="0.9922331583552056in"}

8.  Second, Please fillup "**ITEMS**" details.

![](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="2.2971259842519687in"}

- Once you Click on "**Add**" button then all item will be display from your store. Here, we have select item whichever we want to add for DOORDASH E-Commerce. Then, click on "**Add Items**"button.

![](media/image7.png){width="6.5in" height="2.831470909886264in"}

9.  Now go in "**INFO**" section again and click on the "**SAVE"** button.

![](media/image8.png){width="6.5in" height="3.2257491251093615in"}

10. Once you click on "**SAVE**" button then You will get popup for Succesufull and you can see your **DOORDASH configuration** in list.

![](media/image9.png){width="6.5in" height="2.910376202974628in"}

11. Now from left side Menu, Go into **E-COMMERCE VENDOR** **\>\>** **PROCESS** **\>\>** **ADD**.

![](media/image10.png){width="6.5in" height="2.852884951881015in"}

12. Here, you can select "**DOORDASH**" from Vendor Name and click on "**PROCESS**" button which is available rightside corner.

![](media/image11.png){width="6.5in" height="2.5366491688538932in"}

13. Once you click on "**PROCESS**" button, you can see your file uploading status in "**LIST**" section. (Go into E-COMMERCE VENDOR **\>\>** PROCESS **\>\>** LIST)

![](media/image12.png){width="6.5in" height="2.4278543307086613in"}

14. Please click on "**DOWNLOAD**" button then your file will be download in CSV format.

![](media/image13.png){width="6.5in" height="1.9766754155730533in"}
