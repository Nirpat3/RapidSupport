# How to recall which is I put on hold transaction

**How to recall which is I put on hold transaction?**

You will see recall option next to hold at the bottom on the sales screen.

Select recall.

Click on invoice which one you want to recall (want to finish transaction).

Then, on the right top of the corner, you will see a picture of a floppy sign next to the question mark option. Select the floppy sign.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.895833333333333in" height="4.609375546806649in"}

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.867498906386702in"}

**How to recall I offline invoice which is put on hold?**

- When you have slow internet connectivity or your system is in offline mode due to any server issue. If you hold any transaction, you will be able to see your invoices in offline invoice tab. You will be able to upload your invoice to online mode, once you have proper internet connectivity.

- To upload offline invoice to online invoices, select the invoice from offline invoices tab \> Click on upload button. Your invoice will move to Online invoices tab.

- You can also recall invoices from offline tab, once you get proper network connection. Please note that if you have any network issue, the online tab will not available to recall any invoices.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.918349737532808in"}

- To recall invoices from offline invoices, click on Recall button.

- Click on the Offline invoices tab.

- Select the invoice you wish to recall.

- Click on Floppy option from the top right side corner of the screen.

**How do I delete hold transaction?**

You will see a recall option next to hold at the bottom on the sales screen.

Select recall.

Select invoice below online invoice.

Swipe left side to delete the invoice.

Select the Yes option.
