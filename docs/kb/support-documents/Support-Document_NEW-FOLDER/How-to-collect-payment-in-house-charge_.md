# How to collect payment in house charge_

**How to collect payment in house charge?**

In customer loyalty when customer want to use house charge and there is a negative amount is in house charge and customer want to collect amount then it can be possible from customer loyalty.

- Open Rapid RMS application.

- Go to Cash register module.

- From the top screen, tap on customer icon

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.875in"}

- From customer list, Tap on 'View' icon and go to House charge page.

- Now From House, charge page check 'collect Payment' button and verify if Balance amount is negative (-50) then the button will be Enable mode otherwise it will disable.

- Now If 'Balance Amount' is negative then tap on Collect payment.

- Choose 'Pay full balance' or 'Other Amount' option with amount and tap on 'Enter button.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.527777777777778in"}

- Now you will see, the page will redirect in RCR bill screen with House charge Item.

- Now from RCR screen, complete house charge transaction by any payment type (EX: Cash )
