# How can I logout from Manual entry

**[How can I log out from manual entry?]{.underline}**

- Click on Arrow button to get out from the report on which you are right now.

- It will appear options of manual entry.

- Click on the Logout option available next to the History option.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.680555555555555in"}

- When you click on the Logout option, It will move to **RapidRMS** Dashboard.
