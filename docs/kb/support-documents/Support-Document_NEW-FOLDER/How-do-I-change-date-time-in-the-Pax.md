# How do I change date time in the Pax

**How do I change the date/time in the Pax?**

In the Pax, there are two options to open the main menu: -

> a\) click on the menu. then it will ask you to enter a password. If it will not ask you about entering a password. follow step B or if it will ask you about entering a password skip step B. follow step 2.
>
> b\) hit FUNC and 1 both keys push together then it will ask you to enter a password. Follow step 2.

1.  Enter today's date as a password. for example, today's date is 08/27/2017. You will have to type the 08/27/2017 date. If Pax says the wrong password then try the next day\'s date as a password. For example, tomorrow's date is 08/28/2017 so, you will have to type 08/28/2017.

2.  It will pop up on the main menu. Then, click on the down arrow which is available on the left side of the menu.

3.  You will see the system setting. Then, click on the system setting.

4.  Now, you will see the date/time setup. Then, click on date/time setup

5.  It will pop up on the first date setup. Then, if you want to change the date, hit the yellow button which is available on the left side from the green button. The yellow button helps you to erase the old date.

6.  Then you could type a new date that you want to set up in the Pax.

7.  After you set the date then hit the green button.

8.  Same thing with the time you will have to hit the yellow button which is available on the left side of the green button. It helps you to erase old times and then you could type new times what you want to set up in the Pax.

9.  After set time then hit the green button.

10. Then get out from the Pax menu. Click the red button until you will see Pax\'s main screen.
