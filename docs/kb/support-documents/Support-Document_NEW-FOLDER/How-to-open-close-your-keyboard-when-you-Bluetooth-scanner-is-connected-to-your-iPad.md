# How to open close your keyboard when you Bluetooth scanner is connected to your iPad

**[How to open/close your keyboard when Bluetooth scanner is connected to your iPad?]{.underline}**

- When you use Bluetooth scanner, the scanner works/acts as keyboard in iPad. So when you press the scanner trigger button twice continuously, it will open the apple keyboard in the iPad. Same way you can hide the keyboard by pressing trigger button twice.

- We have a keyboard button in Rapid RMS. That will lock and unlock or open and close the keyboard.
