# How margin is calculated in all reports_

**How is margin calculated in all reports?**

Margin is value in report which reflects profit and cost relationship in terms of %.

User can calculate margin value by following formula.

> • Margin

Profit = net sale - cost of good

Margin= (Profit/ Net sale)\*100

Note: In all report like shift, x, Z history, ZZ history, centralize shift, Centralize Z and in centralized ZZ there is field of margin in available.

For that margin calculation take net sale and cost of good of that particular report and calculate margin.
