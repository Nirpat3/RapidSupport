# How do i e-mail invoice_

**How do I e-mail invoice?**

If the user wants to email invoice detail than the email option is available.

In RCR, there is 2 way to email Invoice.

- In Dashboard, select cash register module and enter 4 digit quick access passwords.

- Ring up items in ring up the screen.

- Now complete transaction by any payment mode.

- In the change screen, you will see 'Email' option for email invoice

> ![](media/image1.png){width="6.125in" height="3.994792213473316in"}

- Now click on Email option and enter user email in email address and click on send button.

> ![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.864831583552056in"}

**Another way**

- In Dashboard, click on cash register module

- In RCR screen, click on top left menu.

- Click on Invoice option.

- In the right side, in invoice list click on any invoice.

- Now you can see invoice detail with Email Option.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.0625in" height="4.380208880139983in"}

- Now click on Email option and enter user email in email address and click on the send button.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.864831583552056in"}
