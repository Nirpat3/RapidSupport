# RapidRms Notification setting_How to OFF

**[How to Stop Notification in RAPID RMS Application]{.underline}**

If you want to stop notification in Rapid RMS Application then follow the below procedure in your iPad (POS).

> **[Step 1]{.underline}** : Click on "**Setting**" option from iPad Home Screen.
>
> ![C:\\Users\\Bhavesh\\Desktop\\STEP 1.png](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.8765879265091865in"}
>
> **[Step 2]{.underline}** : In Setting, you will find "**Rapid RMS**" App icon, click on that icon and you can able to see "**Notifications**" option in right side.
>
> ![C:\\Users\\Bhavesh\\Desktop\\STEP 2.png](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.8765879265091865in"}
>
> **[Step 3]{.underline}** : After click on "**Notifications**", you will find currently ON switch for "**Allow Notifications**."
>
> ![C:\\Users\\Bhavesh\\Desktop\\STEP 3.png](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.8765879265091865in"}

**[Step 4]{.underline}** : Please **OFF** that "**Allow Notification**" Switch.

![C:\\Users\\Bhavesh\\Desktop\\STEP 4.png](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.8765879265091865in"}
