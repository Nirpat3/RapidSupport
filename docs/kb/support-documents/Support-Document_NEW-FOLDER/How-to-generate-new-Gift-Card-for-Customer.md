# How to generate new Gift Card for Customer

**[How to generate new Gift Card for Customer ?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to **RapidRMS Application** with your credentials.

Enter your Email OR User ID.

Enter your Password.

Click on SIGN IN button.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="3.008707349081365in"}

2.  Go into "**CASH REGISTER - RCR**".

Ener your quick password and Click on "**ENTER**" button.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9843832020997376in"}

3.  At the bottom bar, thare is one option "**MORE**", Click on it.

In more, click on "**GIFTCARD**" option.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="3.8024300087489062in"}

4.  In GiftCard, you will find 2 options (Check Balance & Load Balance)

Please click on "**Load Balance**" option to create new Gift Card.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="3.8986843832020996in"}

5.  Click on "**Generate**" button to generate new gift card number.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="4.035513998250218in"}

6.  Once you click on generate button then gift card number will be generate automatically, you need to just enter amount and click on "**SAVE**" button.

![](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="3.8172681539807525in"}

7.  Once you click on "**SAVE**" button, it will ask you with confirmation message, click on "**YES**".

![](media/image7.png){width="6.5in" height="4.535212160979878in"}

8.  Once you click on "**YES**", it will redirect to ringup screen.

You can finish your transcation with any payment option like (**Cash, Debit, Credit**) except RapidGiftCard as you are creating new gift card.

![](media/image8.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9453215223097113in"}

![](media/image9.png){width="6.5in" height="4.503953412073491in"}
