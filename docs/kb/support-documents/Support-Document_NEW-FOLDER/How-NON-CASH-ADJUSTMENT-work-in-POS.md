# How NON-CASH ADJUSTMENT work in POS

**[How NON-CASH Adjustment Work in POS?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to **RapidRMS Application** with your credentials.

- Enter your Email OR User ID.

- Enter your Password.

- Click on SIGN IN button.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="3.008707349081365in"}

2.  Click on "**SETTINGS**".

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="3.313840769903762in"}

3.  Ener your quick password and Click on "**ENTER**" button.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="3.5288123359580053in"}

4.  Click on "**TENDER CONFIGURATION**" & Please ON toggle button for "**Non Cash Adjustment**".

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="3.5470778652668415in"}

5.  Now we will configure Non-Cash Adjustment charges/discount from **INVENTORY MANAGEMENT**.

Ener your quick password and Click on "**ENTER**" button.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="3.3301695100612423in"}

![](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="3.760482283464567in"}

6.  Tap menu ![hamburger-menu-icon-png-white-24.jpg](media/image7.png){width="0.2075404636920385in" height="0.2075404636920385in"} (located on the top left side corner) & Click on "**NON CASH ADJUSTMENT**" option.

![](media/image8.png){width="6.5in" height="3.6084634733158354in"}

7.  Click on **"+ ADD**" option to creat new non-cash adjustment charges/discount.

![](media/image9.png){width="6.5in" height="3.706851487314086in"}

8.  Here, You can create with multiple combination using "**Charge / Discount** " & "**Fix / Amount Range**".

![](media/image10.png){width="5.504166666666666in" height="1.7597222222222222in"}

9.  For Example, Lets creating "**Credit Card Charges**" with Fix & Percantage % charge type (2.25%).

Enter NAME : Credit Card Charges.

10. Please select "Fix" Charge option and Enter your 2.25 in Percantage (%) option, then click on "**SAVE**" button.

![](media/image11.png){width="6.5in" height="4.534004811898512in"}

![](media/image12.png){width="6.5in" height="4.561348425196851in"}

![](media/image13.png){width="6.5in" height="4.059522090988627in"}

11. Once you click on "**SAVE**" button then you will get popup message with "**Non Cash Adjustment inserted successfully**".

![](media/image14.png){width="6.5in" height="4.216540901137358in"}

12. Now, you can see your Non-Cash Adjustment Charges (**Credit Card Charges**) created, please click on "**\>**" from right side to add for payment mode.

![](media/image15.png){width="6.5in" height="3.2811220472440943in"}

13. Go into "**Selected Payment Modes**" & Click on "**Add Payment Mode**".

![](media/image16.png){width="6.5in" height="4.2088265529308835in"}

![](media/image17.png){width="6.5in" height="3.970334645669291in"}

14. Now, Please tick against the "**CREDIT**" Payment to assigned Non-Cash adjustment charges, then click on "**SAVE**" button.

![](media/image18.png){width="6.5in" height="3.336784776902887in"}

15. Once you click on "**SAVE**" button then you will get popup message with "**Non Cash Adjustment updated successfully**".

![](media/image19.png){width="6.5in" height="3.8884984689413824in"}

16. For your information, You can create your Non-Cash Adjustment charges/discount with other combination also.

For Example, you can create with Dicount & Amount Range combination.

![](media/image20.png){width="6.5in" height="3.7847430008748906in"}

![](media/image21.png){width="6.5in" height="3.544455380577428in"}

17. Now, we can do the sales transcation and see the charges effect in credit payment on tender screen.

For Example, In Cash Register (RCR) lets ringup one item with \$100 amount (Total \$105 + \$100 + \$5 tax).

![](media/image22.png){width="6.498472222222222in" height="3.4879997812773404in"}

18. You can see here, Total invoice amount is \$105 including tax and **\$2.36 is non-cash adjustment charges** against the credit tender.

![](media/image23.png){width="6.4991666666666665in" height="3.7120002187226597in"}
