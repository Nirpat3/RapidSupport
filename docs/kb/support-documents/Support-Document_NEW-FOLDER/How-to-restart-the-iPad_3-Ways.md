# How to restart the iPad_3 Ways

**[How to restart the iPad?]{.underline}**

[There are 3 ways to restart [OR]{.underline} turn off/On the iPad.]{.mark}

[[1^st^ Way]{.underline}]{.mark}

1.  [There is a menu in the iPad, Click on the "**SETTINGS**" icon.]{.mark}

![](media/image8.png){width="6.270833333333333in" height="7.880208880139983in"}

2.  [In Settings, You will find "**GENERAL**". In General, You will find the "**SHUTDOWN**" option, Click on Shutdown.]{.mark}

![](media/image5.png){width="6.267716535433071in" height="8.347222222222221in"}

[]{.mark}

3.  [Once you click on ShutDown then the "**Power**]{.mark} **Off Slider**" appears.

4.  Drag the slider, and then wait 30 seconds for your device to turn off.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.270833333333333in" height="7.295080927384077in"}

5.  To turn your device back on, press and hold the top button until you see the Apple logo.

[2nd Way]{.underline}

## **How to restart an iPad without a Home button**

> 1\. Press and hold "Volume Button" & "Top Button" together until the power off slider appears.
>
> ![](media/image2.png){width="6.267716535433071in" height="3.75in"}
>
> 2\. Drag the slider, and then wait 30 seconds for your device to turn off.
>
> 3\. To turn your device back on, press and hold the top button until you see the Apple logo.

## **How to restart an iPad with a Home button**

> 1\. Press and hold the "Top Button\'\' until the power off slider appears.
>
> ![](media/image3.png){width="6.267716535433071in" height="3.75in"}
>
> 2\. Drag the slider, and then wait for 30 seconds for your device to turn off.
>
> 3\. To turn your device back on, press and hold the top button until you see the Apple logo.

[3rd Way]{.underline}

> 1\. Click on the "**Switch Control**" icon on iPad.
>
> ![](media/image4.png){width="6.267716535433071in" height="7.722222222222222in"}
>
> 2\. After clicking on switch control, you will find the "**Device**" option.
>
> ![](media/image6.png){width="6.267716535433071in" height="8.347222222222221in"}
>
> 3\. After clicking on the device, you will find the "**More**" option.
>
> ![](media/image7.png){width="6.267716535433071in" height="8.347222222222221in"}
>
> 4\. After clicking on More option, you will find the "**Restart**" option.
>
> ![](media/image9.png){width="6.267716535433071in" height="8.347222222222221in"}
