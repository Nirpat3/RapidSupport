# How to upload EDI File_UNFI

**[How to upload EDI file ?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to BackOffice with your credentials.

2.  Tap menu ![hamburger-menu-icon-png-white-24.jpg](media/image1.png){width="0.2075404636920385in" height="0.2075404636920385in"} (located on the top left side).

3.  Click on "**INVENTORY**" Section.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="2.484831583552056in"}

4.  In INVENTORY, click on "**EDI FILE**" section.

In LIST, You can able to see your existing uploaded file list.

Now, if you want to upload NEW EDI FILE then click on "**ADD**" option.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="2.850799431321085in"}

5.  Once you click on "**ADD**", you will find 3 options to proceed with your file.

- [Select a File Option]{.underline} : Choose "**File Select**" option.

- [File Type]{.underline} : You can choose your Vendor File Type (For Example, Here I have selected "**UNFI**").

- [Upload File]{.underline} : You can select your EDI File from your computer with the help of "**Choose File**" option.

(**[Special NOTE]{.underline}** : if you have CSV file in your email then just download that file in your computer and please upload directly without opening that file. Because if you open that CSV file then your UPC Barcode will be change due to CSV file format.)

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="2.2640201224846894in"}

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="3.488190069991251in"}

6.  Once you select file then other 3 options will be display for your file.

- [Vendor]{.underline} : You can select your **Vendor Name** from your existing vendor list.

- [Open Order]{.underline} : Here, we have given 2 options (You can **Select Existing Open Order** [OR]{.underline} you can **Create New Open Order**).

- [Invoice]{.underline} : it will autocatch from your vendor file [OR]{.underline} you can put manually as well.

Please click on "**IMPORT**" button.

![](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9845439632545934in"}

7.  Once you click on Import button then below screen will be display.

- Here, you can update your [COST PRICE]{.underline} and [SELLING PRICE]{.underline} by manually.

- You can select [Department]{.underline} as well for particular item.

- Once Your Price and Department updation over then you can click on "**RECONCILE**" button to finish the EDI File Process.

![](media/image7.png){width="6.5in" height="3.2423797025371828in"}

- For your information, ![](media/image8.png){width="0.4798611111111111in" height="0.5597222222222222in"} this button will help you to update your price from bottom to box.

{[You can see price are mentioned at bottom side of every box, that price received from file and if you want to update that price then you can simply click on this button.}]{.underline}

8.  Once you click on Reconcile button then one popup will be open for remarks and then click on "**SAVE**" button.

![](media/image9.png){width="6.5in" height="2.997107392825897in"}

9.  Once you click on "**SAVE**" button, it will finish and added in CLOSE ORDER list.

![](media/image10.png){width="6.5in" height="2.2234984689413824in"}

10. Now, you can check your item on ringup screen in Cash Register.

**[Special NOTE]{.underline}** : if you received your item with PACK details in file then here it will automatically create your single item base on your PACK Qty.

**[For Example]{.underline}** : Please refer below screenshort. Here, we received 12-Qty PACK item with \$49.24 Price. So, single item created with \$4.10 Price (\$49.24 / 12 = \$4.10).

![](media/image11.png){width="6.5in" height="4.492982283464567in"}

![](media/image12.png){width="6.5in" height="4.541143919510061in"}
