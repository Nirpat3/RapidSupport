# How to view Inverntory information in iPad

**How to View Inventory Information in iPad:**

There is an option in RapidRMS to view inventory information like a product number, total stock, total cost, total price, average cost and average price and margin/markup etc.

Here are the steps on how to view it.

1.  Open RapidRMS application in your iPad.

2.  Select Inventory Management from the dashboard.

3.  Enter your valid passcode to access Inventory Management Module.

4.  Click on Info button from the bottom of the screen.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.736111111111111in"}

5.  Total Product count, Total number of Item available, cost and price detail and average information will display.
