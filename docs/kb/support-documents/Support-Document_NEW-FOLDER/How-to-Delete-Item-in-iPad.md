# How to Delete Item in iPad

**How to Delete Item on iPad:**

If you have created an Item that is by mistake created or you don't need that Item in your application then there is delete item functionality in RapidRMS.

Here are the steps on how to delete your Item.

- Open the RapidRMS application on your iPad.

- Select Inventory Management from the dashboard.

- Enter your valid passcode to access Inventory Management Module.

- You can delete an Item from two methods.

**Method 1:**

- Click on an item from the list to delete an existing Item.

- Edit Item screen will open.

- Click on the Delete button from the bottom of the screen.

> ![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.666666666666667in"}

- Click Yes on the Confirmation window to delete the Item.

- Click No if you don't want to delete an Item.

> ![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.722222222222222in"}

**Method 2:**

- Slide right in any Item from the list.

- Option bar with the single, case, pack, copy and delete options available.

> ![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.708333333333333in"}

- Click on the delete button.

- Click Yes on the confirmation window to delete the Item.
