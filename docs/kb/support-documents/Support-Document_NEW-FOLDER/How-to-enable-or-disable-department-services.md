# How to enable or disable department services

**[How to enable or disable department services?]{.underline}**

- Department service is the division of a business that provides different kinds of services to the customer. Some important department services are lottery, gas, money order, gift card, check cash, vendor payout, house charge, cashback, Lottery payout, and payout, etc.

<!-- -->

- The User cannot create or delete any services. The user can only update these services.

**Please follow the below steps to enable or disable department services:-**

- Open Rapid RMS application.

- Select the inventory management module from the dashboard.

- Enter your quick access password.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.777777777777778in"}

- Click on the three-line button from the top left side corner of the screen.

- Select the Department option from the left side menu.

- Select Services tab appears beside the department tab.

- The list of all default services will appear in the services tab.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.708333333333333in"}

- You can enable or disable all services by clicking on the toggle button available next to the record.

- **For Example:-** If you want to use lottery services, click on the toggle button next to Lottery services to enable lottery services. When you click on the toggle button, it will appear in black.

- If you want to disable any services, click on the same toggle button to disable any services.

- Please note that when you enable any service then you can only open that service in edit mode. It is not allowed to delete any services from the department services section.

- Click on services to open in edit mode.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.791666666666667in"}

- Tap on the Save button.

- If you do not want to save any changes, click on the close button.
