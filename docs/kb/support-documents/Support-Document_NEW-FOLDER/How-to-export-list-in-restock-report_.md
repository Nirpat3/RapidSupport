# How to export list in restock report_

**How to export list in restock report?**

In sales history or in restocking if you want to email or print item list then it can be possible by export option.

- Open rapid RMS application.

- Go to restock module.

- Open either sales history or restocking.

- At the bottom bar, there is one option "export".

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.708333333333333in"}

- When applying email then pop up for email will open.

- Enter the proper email address.

- Item list will be sent to entered email address.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.722222222222222in"}

- For preview apply the option to print it will display a preview of sales history/restocking item list.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.833333333333333in"}
