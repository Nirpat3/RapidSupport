# How to Update Deliver Invoice number_

**[How to Update Deliver Invoice number?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

<!-- -->

- Open Rapid RMS application.

- Open purchase order module from the dashboard.

- Tap 'delivery pending' module.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.708333333333333in"}

- In delivery pending all the orders which are move from open order will be listed.

- Orders will display with created date & time, OPN#, title, invoice, delivery date & time, user and Action.

- Open any invoice which invoice number you want to change.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.777777777777778in"}

- In Delivery Screen, on top screen you will see invoice no.

- Tap on the invoice number.

- Now you will see 'delivery invoice number' popup.

- Change 'delivery invoice number' and click on the Save button.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.763888888888889in"}

- Now in delivery screen page click on save button.

- Now you will see, the Delivery invoice number will update.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.708333333333333in"}
