# How to set tax on price

**[How to set a tax on price?]{.underline}**

- There are two options to apply tax on item price.

1.  **[Original Price:-]{.underline}** When you select the original price option, it will apply tax on the retail price of an item. Even if you have given a discount on an item, Tax will apply on retail price only.

2.  **[Discount Price:]{.underline}-** When you select Discounted price option, it will apply tax on the Discounted price of an item.

- Open rapid RMS application.

- Click on the setting option from the bottom of the screen.

- Select the tender configuration option from the list.

<!-- -->

- It will appear the Apply taxes on option at the right side corner of the screen.

- .Select the Original price radio button.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.352277996500438in"}

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.694444444444445in"}

- As you can see in the above image, the User has given a discount, but tax is applied to the retail price of an item instead of a discounted item of an item.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.777777777777778in"}

- Now, select Discounted price option from the tender configuration option.

- Go to the cash register and ring up the item.

- Apply discount for the same.

- When you select Discounted price option, Tax applies on the discounted price of an item.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.791666666666667in"}

- As you can see in the above image, Tax is applied to the discounted price of an item, however,

- The tax amount will change as per the discount applied on an item or on the total bill amount accordingly.
