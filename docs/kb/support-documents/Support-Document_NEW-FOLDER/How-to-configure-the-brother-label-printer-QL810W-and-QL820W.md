# How to configure the brother label printer QL810W and QL820W

**[How to configure the brother label printer (QL810w, QL820w)?]{.underline}**

- Before connecting the brother printer to POS, we need to configure the brother printer with the router.

Do one of the following:

- [If you have a wireless router/access point](#PC_connect)

- [If you do not have a wireless router/access point](#Without_Wireless_Connection)

<!-- -->

- []{#PC_connect .anchor}**If you have a wireless router/access point**

There are two ways to configure a printer with a wireless router/access point.

#### [Connect using a wireless router/access point (WPS)](#if-your-wireless-routeraccess-point-supports-wps-wi-fi-protected-setup-do-the-following)

#### [Connect the printer to the wireless router/access point using your computer](#Using_Computer)

#### If your wireless router/access point supports WPS (Wi-Fi Protected Setup™), do the following:

1.  First, confirm that your wireless router/access point has the WPS symbol, and place the printer close to the wireless router/access point.

2.  Turn on the printer.

3.  If you have a **QL-810W** printer, press and hold the **WPS** button for one second.

4.  If you have a **QL-820NWB** printer, press the **Menu** button and then![ArrowDown](media/image1.gif){width="0.16319444444444445in" height="0.15208333333333332in"} button to select the **WLAN** menu, and then press the **OK** button. Press the![ArrowDown](media/image1.gif){width="0.16319444444444445in" height="0.15208333333333332in"}button to select the **WPS Button Push** menu, and then press the **OK** button.

5.  Press the WPS button on your WLAN router/access point.

6.  When the connection is established, the Wi-Fi![https://support.brother.com/g/b/img/faqend/faqp00100064_004/us/en/2290431/Wi-Fibutton.gif](media/image2.gif){width="0.1736111111111111in" height="0.1736111111111111in"} LED is lit blue or the WLAN![https://support.brother.com/g/b/img/faqend/faqp00100064_004/us/en/2290431/Wi-Fibutton.gif](media/image2.gif){width="0.1736111111111111in" height="0.1736111111111111in"} icon appears on the LCD.

7.  Connect your POS device to the wireless router/access point.  (You can skip this step if your device is already connected to a Wi-Fi network.)

To connect the Printer to the POS device, click here!

2)  []{#Using_Computer .anchor}**Connect the printer to the wireless router/access point using your computer, do the following:**

<!-- -->

1.  Connect your computer to your wireless router/access point.

2.  Connect your printer to the same wireless router/access point.

3.  Connect your printer and computer.

4.  Connect your POS device to the same wireless router/access point. (You can skip this step if your device is already connected to a Wi-Fi network.)

If you have a **Window**, Click [here](#Window)!

If you have a **Mac**, Click [here!](#Mac)

- []{#Window .anchor}**For Window**

<!-- -->

- Before you begin, confirm the SSID (Network Name) and the password (Network Key) of your wireless router/access point.

1.  Download the printer driver from the [**[Downloads]{.underline}**](https://support.brother.com/g/b/link.aspx?content=dl) section of this website. 

2.  Double-click the downloaded file to begin the installation.

3.  Click **Next**.

![https://support.brother.com/g/b/img/faqend/faqp00100064_004/us/en/2290433/driver1_en.gif](media/image3.gif){width="5.20625in" height="3.6847222222222222in"}

4.  When the screen for selecting the connection method appears, select **Wireless Network Connection**, and then click **Next**.

![https://support.brother.com/g/b/img/faqend/faqp00100064_004/us/en/2290434/driver2_en.gif](media/image4.gif){width="3.5868055555555554in" height="2.7720352143482065in"}

5.  Select **Brother Peer-to-Peer Network Printer** and then click **Next**.

![https://support.brother.com/g/b/img/faqend/faqp00100064_004/us/en/2290435/driver3_en.gif](media/image5.gif){width="5.20625in" height="3.6847222222222222in"}

6.  Select **Change the Firewall port settings to enable network connection and continue with the installation. (Recommended)**, and then click **Next**.

![https://support.brother.com/g/b/img/faqend/faqp00100064_004/us/en/2290436/driver4_en.gif](media/image6.gif){width="5.20625in" height="3.6847222222222222in"}

7.  Prepare the USB cable, select **Yes, I have a USB cable to use for installation**, and then click **Next**.\
    Follow the on-screen instructions.

![https://support.brother.com/g/b/img/faqend/faqp00100064_004/us/en/2290437/driver5_en.gif](media/image7.gif){width="5.20625in" height="3.597916666666667in"}

8.  The wizard will search for wireless networks available to your printer.

9.  Select the SSID (Network Name) noted beforehand, and then click **Next**.

![https://support.brother.com/g/b/img/faqend/faqp00100064_004/us/en/2290438/driver6_en.gif](media/image8.gif){width="5.20625in" height="3.597916666666667in"}

10. Confirm the wireless network settings, and then click \[**Next**\]. The settings will be sent to your printer.

![https://support.brother.com/g/b/img/faqend/faqp00100064_004/us/en/2290439/driver7_en.gif](media/image9.gif){width="5.20625in" height="3.597916666666667in"}

11. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the software installation.

12. When the installation is complete, click \[**Finish**\] to close the installer window.

13. When the printer is connected to your wireless router/access point, the Wi-Fi LED lit in blue or the Wi-Fi![https://support.brother.com/g/b/img/faqend/faqp00100064_004/us/en/2290431/Wi-Fibutton.gif](media/image2.gif){width="0.1736111111111111in" height="0.1736111111111111in"} icon appears on the LCD.

14. Connect your POS device to the wireless router/access point.  (You can skip this step if your device is already connected to a Wi-Fi network)

[]{#Mac .anchor}**For Mac**

- Before you begin, confirm the SSID (Network Name) and the password (Network Key) of your wireless router/access point.

1.  Download the Full Software Package from the [**[Downloads]{.underline}**](https://support.brother.com/g/b/link.aspx?content=dl) section of this website.

2.  Double-click the downloaded file.

3.  Open the **Utility** folder and find the **Wireless Device Setup Wizard**.

4.  Double-click **Wireless Device Setup Wizard. app**.

5.  Follow the on-screen instructions to configure the wireless settings between the printer and your wireless router/access point.

- We recommend using a USB cable to complete the connection.

6.  When selecting the wireless router/access point, make sure you select the SSID that you confirmed previously.

7.  Connect your POS device to the wireless router/access point. If you are already connected to a Wi-Fi network.

8.  Select the SSID that you used previously. The connection between your device and your wireless router/access point will be established.

- []{#Without_Wireless_Connection .anchor}**If you do not have a wireless router/access point**

<!-- -->

- You can connect the printer using Wireless Direct. If your printer uses the latest firmware, \"Wireless Direct\" is already selected.

**Please follow the below instructions:-**

1.  Confirm the Wireless Direct settings of the printer from the computer using the Printer Setting Tool, or from the printer (QL-820NWB only).

- **Specifying the settings from the computer using the Printer Setting Tool:**

<!-- -->

- **For Windows**

1.  Connect the printer and computer with a USB cable.

2.  Start the Printer Setting Tool on your computer, and click Communication Settings.

3.  Select I**nfrastructure and Wireless Direct** or **Wireless Direct** for **Selected Interface** in **Communication Settings** in the **General** tab, and confirm the **Wireless Direct** tab.

- **For Mac**

  1.  Connect the printer and computer with a USB cable.

  2.  Launch the Printer Setting Tool on your computer.

  3.  Click the **Wireless LAN** tab.

  4.  Confirm that **Wireless Direct** is set to **On**

<!-- -->

- **Specifying the settings from the printer (QL-820NWB only):**

  1.  Press the **Menu** and![ArrowDown](media/image1.gif){width="0.16319444444444445in" height="0.15208333333333332in"} buttons to select the **WLAN** menu, and then press the **OK** button. Set **WLAN (On/Off)** to **On**.

  2.  Press the **Menu** and![ArrowDown](media/image1.gif){width="0.16319444444444445in" height="0.15208333333333332in"} buttons to select the **WLAN** menu, and then press the **OK** button.

  3.  Press the![arrow_down](media/image1.gif){width="0.16319444444444445in" height="0.15208333333333332in"}button to select the **Network Mode** menu, and then press the **OK** button.

  4.  Select **Direct Mode**, and then press the **OK** button. The Wireless Direct icon appears on the LCD.

2.  Turn on the Brother Label printer.

3.  If you have a **QL-810W** printer, Press and hold the **Wi-Fi** ![Wi-Fi](media/image10.gif){width="0.22847222222222222in" height="0.13055555555555556in"} button on the printer for one second to turn the Wi-Fi function On. The Wi-Fi LED starts flashing blue every three seconds.

4.  **If you have QL-820NWB**: Press the **Menu b**utton and then![ArrowDown](media/image1.gif){width="0.16319444444444445in" height="0.15208333333333332in"} button to select the **WLAN** menu, and set **WLAN (On/Off)** to **On**.
