# How can I hold invoice from Manual entry

**[How can I hold the invoice from Manual entry?]{.underline}**

- There are two ways to put an invoice on Hold.

1)  Put on hold a new invoice.

2)  Put hold an existing invoice from the Recall option.

- **Put on hold a new invoice:-**

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.823849518810149in"}

- After creating a new invoice and adding an item from the search option, you can hold a particular invoice on hold.

- Click on the search button from the bottom bar.

- Select items from the list.

- Click on add button from the bottom bar.

- To hold an invoice, click on the hold button from the bottom bar.

- The invoice will move the recall option. You will be able to review your hold invoice from the 'Recall' option.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.594654418197726in"}

- **Put hold an existing invoice from recall option:-**

<!-- -->

- Open the manual entry module from the dashboard.

- Click on the Recall option.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.375022965879265in"}

- All hold invoices list will appear in the Recall.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.001288276465441in"}

- Click on any invoice from the list.

- It will prompt a confirmation message

- Select the 'Yes' option.

- Update details you want to update.

- At the bottom, click on the hold button.
