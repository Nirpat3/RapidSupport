# How to manage discount in item

**How to manage discount in item**

You can provide quantity based discount in your items. You can also apply department Tax on your specified discount. You can set one or more discounts on one item.

Here are the steps on how to manage an item discount.

- Open RapidRMS application in your iPad.

- Select Inventory Management from the dashboard.

- Enter your valid passcode to access Inventory Management Module.

- Click on New Item from the bottom of the page to add new Item or click on an item from the list to edit existing Item.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.708333333333333in"}

- Add/Edit Item screen will open.

- In the top of the screen, it has Info, Pricing, Discount, History and Options tabs.

- Click on Discount tab from the top of the screen.

- Set "No POS Discount" to ON if you don't want to apply a discount on Item at selling time. Default the option is set to OFF.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.722222222222222in"}

- Click on "Use a quantity discount pricing table" option. Discount Detail table will open.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.722222222222222in"}

- Specify Item quantity and Item Price.

- Click on Add New button if you want to add more discounts.

- Click on cancel button if you want to delete discount.

- Click on **Save** button from the bottom of the page to save the detail.
