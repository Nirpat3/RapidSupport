# How to turn on display age verification data in receipt option from settings

**[How to turn on display age verification data in the receipt option from settings?]{.underline}**

- The Cashier can also view the entered or verified Date of birth of the customer on the Sales Receipt.

1.  Click on the settings option ![C:\\Users\\mit shah\\AppData\\Local\\Microsoft\\Windows\\INetCache\\IE\\BU8Y79T3\\WMF-Agora-Settings_BCBCBC.svg\[1\].png](media/image1.png){width="0.17463910761154855in" height="0.17463910761154855in"} available at the bottom of the DashBoard.

![C:\\Users\\mit shah\\Downloads\\MicrosoftTeams-image (8).png](media/image2.jpeg){width="6.268055555555556in" height="3.65541447944007in"}

2.  Enter your quick access password.

3.  Under Rapid Settings, click on options.

4.  Turn on the toggle button for Display age verification data in the receipt option.

![C:\\Users\\mit shah\\Documents\\Lightshot\\Screenshot_33.png](media/image3.png){width="6.2720395888014in" height="3.278260061242345in"}

- As you can see in the below image, the entered or verified date of birth of our customer appears below the item description in the receipt.
