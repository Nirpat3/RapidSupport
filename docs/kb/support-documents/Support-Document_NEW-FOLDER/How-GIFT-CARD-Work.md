# How GIFT CARD Work

**[How GIFT CARD work ?]{.underline}**

- First, We need to activate GIFT CARD Services either from BackOffice [OR]{.underline} POS.

- Second, We need to create Payment Type for executing transcation through GiftCard.

- Third, We need to generate New Gift Card for customer.

- Forth, We can generate sales invoices by collecing payment through Gift Card.

**[How can we activate GIFT CARD Service from BackOffice ?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to BackOffice with your credentials.

2.  Tap menu ![hamburger-menu-icon-png-white-24.jpg](media/image1.png){width="0.2075404636920385in" height="0.2075404636920385in"} (located on the top left side)

3.  Click on "**INVENTORY**" Section.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="2.484831583552056in"}

4.  In INVENTORY, click on "**DEPARTMENT**" option.

In Department, You will find "Departmet Setting" option.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="1.2338987314085739in"}

5.  In Department Setting, You can ON Gift Card Services.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="3.274475065616798in"}

**[How can we activate GIFT CARD Service from POS ?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to **RapidRMS Application** with your credentials.

Enter your Email OR User ID.

Enter your Password.

Click on SIGN IN button.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="3.008707349081365in"}

2.  Click on "**INVENTORY MANAGEMENT**".

Ener your quick password and Click on "**ENTER**" button.

![](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="3.3301695100612423in"}

![](media/image7.png){width="6.5in" height="3.760482283464567in"}

3.  Tap menu ![hamburger-menu-icon-png-white-24.jpg](media/image1.png){width="0.2075404636920385in" height="0.2075404636920385in"} (located on the top left side corner) & Click on "**DEPARTMENT**" option.

![](media/image8.png){width="6.5in" height="3.6404669728783903in"}

4.  In Department, You will find "**SERVICES**" option, Click on it.

In Services, You can please toggle ON for "**Gift Card**".

![](media/image9.png){width="6.5in" height="3.560456036745407in"}

**[How to create GiftCard Payment Type in BackOffice ?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to BackOffice with your credentials.

2.  Tap menu ![hamburger-menu-icon-png-white-24.jpg](media/image1.png){width="0.2075404636920385in" height="0.2075404636920385in"} (located on the top left side)

3.  Click on "**SETTINGS**" Section.

In Settings, Click on "**STORE**".

In Store, Click on "**PAYMENT TYPE**".

![](media/image10.png){width="6.5in" height="2.6006113298337707in"}

4.  In Payment Type, Click on "**ADD**" to create new payment type.

![](media/image11.png){width="6.5in" height="2.048931539807524in"}

5.  Please fillup all details to create "**GIFT CARD**" payment type.

[Payment Name]{.underline} : GIFT CARD

[Payment Code]{.underline} : Any code as per your requirement

[Payment Type]{.underline} : Please select "RapidRMS Gift Card" with the help of scroll arrow.

[Drop Amount]{.underline} : its optional.

[Force Drop Amount]{.underline} : its optional.

Click on "SAVE" button.

![](media/image12.png){width="6.5in" height="2.387494531933508in"}

6.  Once you click on "SAVE", you can see your "GIFT CARD" in your payment type list.

![](media/image13.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9324660979877515in"}

**[How to generate new Gift Card for Customer ?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to **RapidRMS Application** with your credentials.

Enter your Email OR User ID.

Enter your Password.

Click on SIGN IN button.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="3.008707349081365in"}

2.  Go into "**CASH REGISTER - RCR**".

Ener your quick password and Click on "**ENTER**" button.

![](media/image14.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9843832020997376in"}

3.  At the bottom bar, thare is one option "**MORE**", Click on it.

In more, click on "**GIFTCARD**" option.

![](media/image15.png){width="6.5in" height="3.8024300087489062in"}

4.  In GiftCard, you will find 2 options (Check Balance & Load Balance)

Please click on "**Load Balance**" option to create new Gift Card.

![](media/image16.png){width="6.5in" height="3.8986843832020996in"}

5.  Click on "**Generate**" button to generate new gift card number.

![](media/image17.png){width="6.5in" height="4.035513998250218in"}

6.  Once you click on generate button then gift card number will be generate automatically, you need to just enter amount and click on "**SAVE**" button.

![](media/image18.png){width="6.5in" height="3.8172681539807525in"}

7.  Once you click on "**SAVE**" button, it will ask you with confirmation message, click on "**YES**".

![](media/image19.png){width="6.5in" height="4.535212160979878in"}

8.  Once you click on "**YES**", it will redirect to ringup screen.

You can finish your transcation with any payment option like (**Cash, Debit, Credit**) except RapidGiftCard as you are creating new gift card.

![](media/image20.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9453215223097113in"}

![](media/image21.png){width="6.5in" height="4.503953412073491in"}

**[How to generate Sales Invoices through Gift Card Payment ?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to **RapidRMS Application** with your credentials.

Enter your Email OR User ID.

Enter your Password.

Click on SIGN IN button.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="2.7769488188976377in"}

2.  Go into "**CASH REGISTER - RCR**". Ener your quick password and Click on "**ENTER**" button.

3.  Ringup item's that customer wants to buy, Now Click on "**DONE**" button and proceed for the payment on Tender Screen.

![](media/image22.png){width="6.5in" height="3.3451443569553807in"}

4.  Here, you can select "**GIFT CARD**" tender to collect payment and click on "**DONE**" button. It will redirect to RMS Gift Card Screen.

![](media/image23.png){width="6.5in" height="3.8244903762029745in"}

5.  In RMS Gift Card Screen, Please enter Customer "**Gift Card Number"** and Click on "**SEND**" button.

![](media/image24.png){width="6.5in" height="3.84205927384077in"}

6.  Click on "**DONE**" button and finish your transaction.

![](media/image25.png){width="6.5in" height="4.525629921259842in"}
