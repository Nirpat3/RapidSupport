# How to use the RapidRMS Support Channel

**[How to use the RapidRMS Support Channel?]{.underline}**

- **What is Support Channel?**

<!-- -->

- A support channel is a medium through which communication between you and our Live agent is done.

- **RapidRMS** support channel provides live change support whereas our **Liveagent** help you to solve your query or concern in a few minutes.

**Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Open RapidRMS Application.

2.  Click on the question mark '**?'** option available on the top left side corner of the screen.

![](media/image1.png){width="5.5in" height="4.095403543307087in"}

3.  Click on the Message option.

4.  Write down your query that you are facing in your system.

![](media/image2.png){width="5.5in" height="4.127074584426946in"}

5.  Click on **Send** button.

6.  If you want to insert a screenshot to share with our Live Agent.

7.  Click on the Image Icon next to the GIF icon.

![](media/image3.png){width="5.5in" height="4.119827209098863in"}

8.  Select the image.

![](media/image4.png){width="5.5in" height="4.147415791776028in"}

9.  Click on **Send** button.

10. If you have already sent a message to our Live agent and your conversation is opened. Click on a conversation to open your pending conversation.

![](media/image5.png){width="5.5in" height="4.14741469816273in"}

11. If you want to review your pending conversation, select conversation option from Dashboard.
