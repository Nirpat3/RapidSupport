# Verifone Commander_Fuel Configuration in RapidRMS Application

**[Verifone Commander Fuel Configuration Process in RapidRMS Application]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to RapidRMS Application with your credentials.

- Enter your "**Email OR User ID**".

- Enter your "**Password**".

- Click on "**SIGN IN**" button.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9370034995625547in"}

2.  Click on "**SETTINGS**" option.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="3.273818897637795in"}

3.  Enter your quick password and Click on "**ENTER**" button.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="3.312424540682415in"}

4.  Click on the "**DISPENSER SETTINGS**" option.

In Dispenser Settings, Click on the "**Verifone Commander**" option.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.285398075240595in"}

5.  In Verifone Commander,

- We have to fillup this information.

> For Example,
>
> [Commander IP Address]{.underline} : 192.168.14.11
>
> [Commander User Name]{.underline} : manager, [Commander Password]{.underline} : A123456
>
> [Workstation]{.underline} : 63 (It will fill automatically)

- After that, Please click on "**CONFIGURE**" button.

- You will get status as "**Connected**" in Green colour.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="3.025722878390201in"}

6.  you will get popup message that "**Configuration Successfully Uploaded**".

![](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="2.71292760279965in"}
