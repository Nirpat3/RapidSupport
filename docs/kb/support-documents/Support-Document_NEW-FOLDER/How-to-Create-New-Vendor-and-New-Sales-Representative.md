# How to Create New Vendor and New Sales Representative

**[How to Create New Vendor and New Sales Representative?]{.underline}**

- Open the RapidRMS application on your iPad.

- Select Inventory Management from the dashboard.

- Enter your quick access password

- Click on add button to create a new Item from the bottom of the page or click on any item from the list to edit an existing Item.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.75in"}

- Add/Edit Item screen will open.

- There is a vendor option available in the below tax section. You may need to scroll down.

- The vendor section is available under the Info tab.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.856231408573929in"}

- Click on "\>" option to open vendor list.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.722222222222222in"}

- Click on Add New Vendor button from the bottom of the screen.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.736111111111111in"}

- Fill in vendor necessary details and click on the save button. The added vendor will appear in the list.

- If you want to add sales representative detail, first select a vendor from the list.

- There will appear \> sign on the right side of selected vendor from the list.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="4.736111111111111in"}

- Click on the \> option. The sales Representative window will open.

![](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="4.736111111111111in"}

- Click on Add button to create new representative from the bottom of the page.

![](media/image7.png){width="6.5in" height="4.736111111111111in"}

- Fill up the necessary details and click on the Save button. The added sales representative will appear on the list.

- If you want to select the sales representative for the vendor, Select the sales representative and click on done buton.

- If you do not want to select sales representative, click on close buton.

- It will appear vendor list again.

- Please unselect the vendor from the list.

- click on close button again.

- Now, click on \<- Back arrow button from the top left side corner of the screen.
