# How to increase quantity for ring up items

**[How to increase the quantity for ring up items?]{.mark}**

- [Follow the below steps to increase the quantity for ring up items.]{.mark}

<!-- -->

- [Open Rapidrms Application.]{.mark}

- [Go to cash Register module.]{.mark}

- [Ring up Items.]{.mark}

- [In bill screen, tap on that item's quantity which you want to change or increase.]{.mark}

- [Enter the item quantity and click on]{.mark}![](media/image1.png){width="0.875in" height="0.20833333333333334in"} [button.]{.mark}

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.888888888888889in"}

- [Now in bill screen, you will see changed quantity in that item.]{.mark}

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.888888888888889in"}
