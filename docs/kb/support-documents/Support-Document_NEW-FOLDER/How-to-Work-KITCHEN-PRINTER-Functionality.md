# **[Kitchen Department]{.underline}** 

- Note : We need to create specific Department according to your available Kitchen in your store.

- For Example, If you have Veg Food Kitchen then you can create one specific Department like "VEG KITCHEN".

And, if you have Non-Veg Food Kitchen then you can create one specific Department like "NON-VEG KITCHEN".

- Please refer support document.

## **[How to connect Kitchen Printer with POS ?]{.underline}**

1.  Login to POS with your user credentials.

2.  Click on "**SETTING**".

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="3.3229166666666665in"}

3.  Click on "**KITCHEN PRINTER**" and Your available Printer will be display with IP address in "AVAILABLE PRINTERS" section.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="3.5208333333333335in"}

4.  Once you click on your Printer then **Printer Setting** will be open.

- Please give name to your Printer.

(For Example : Veg Kitchen Printer)

- Please select Department from "**\>**".

(For Example : Here, we have selected "VEG KITCHEN" department for this printer)

- Click on "**SAVE**" button.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.5367027559055115in"}

5.  Now, You can see your printer added in list of "**ADDED TO THIS DEVICE**" section.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.516948818897638in"}

**[NOTE]{.underline}** : This way, you can add multiple printer for your different kind of Kitchen.

# **[How Kitchen Printer Work in RCR (Cash Register) ?]{.underline}**

1.  You can ringup your item from "VEG KITCHEN" Department.

2.  Now if you want to send these items order to your kitchen then please clikc on "SEND ORDER" button, which is available at bottom side of ringup screen.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="3.982272528433946in"}

3.  Item order will be print in that particular kitchen printer.
