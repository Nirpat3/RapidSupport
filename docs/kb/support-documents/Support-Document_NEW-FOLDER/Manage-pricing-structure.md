# Manage pricing structure

**How to manage the price structure of the item in inventory management**?

You can manage the cost and price of your item with margin/markup for single, item case-wise, and item pack-wise. You can also set different levels of pricing based on your conditions and apply them accordingly. You can also specify your initial Item stock when creating a new item.

- Open the RapidRMS application on your iPad.

- Select Inventory Management from the dashboard.

- Enter your valid passcode to access Inventory Management Module.

- Click on New Item from the bottom of the page to add a new Item or click on an item from the list to edit an existing Item.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.541666666666667in"}

- Add/Edit Item screen will open.

- At the top of the screen, it has Info, Pricing, Discount, History, and Options tabs.

- In the Info tab, it has the Pricing section. In the pricing section Cost, Markup/Margin, Price and \# of Qty fields are available. If we set "Remove Inventory Counter for Qty OH" to off from the Options tab then the Qty OH file is available. By default, the option is set to ON.

Following is a pricing table in which item price is possible to add and change.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.763888888888889in"}

- In the above table price and the cost is added in three different ways. Single, case, and pack.

- **\# of qty**: It is a field for qty enter that you want to define. In single by default value is 1. You cannot change \# of qty for single. Case and pack are user define. How many items that you want to put in one bundle or bunch is entered in the case and pack. The value of the case and pack must not same. For example value of \# of qty for the case could be 5 and the pack could be 7.

- **Price**: Price in single indicates the price of that single item qty. And price in the case and pack indicate that price of qty defines in the case and pack.

- **Cost**: Cost in single indicate the cost value of the single item. And cost in the case and pack indicates the total cost of qty available in the case and pack.

- **Margin/ mark up**: Margin/mark up is profit calculation according to price and cost. You can set the margin and mark up both values. According to that price and cost for single, case and pack will affect.

- **Qty OH**: Qty OH is a figure of an item that is available in-store at present. Also, you can consider Qty OH as a stock of items available in-store. Default Case and Pack fields are non-editable.

- According to the value of \# of qty for case and pack, Qty OH will set.

**Pricing level**

You can set pricing levels by defining various prices for a single case and pack. The following is a structure for the price level.

- Open any item in edit mode.

- Go to the pricing tab.

- Define price level.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.722222222222222in"}

- Here in this table, all values of price and cost will reflect from the pricing table. Also, you can change the value from here.

- You can define different price levels for the same item for single, case, and pack.

- For example for a single if price level A is selected that means in the POS screen price of a single item will consider \$5.00, not \$7.00.

**Notification button**

- Note that there is one button for the case and the pack.

- When buttons are off then no reflection of the case and pack in the POS screen when the item is searched by a single barcode. But when buttons are ON that means in the POS screen when the item is searched by a single barcode then a pop-up window will display for case and pack.
