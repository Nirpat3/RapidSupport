# Why sometimes application slow running what I do

**Why sometimes application slow running what I do?**

- It may have system update notification in the iPad setting.

- You will have to go to setting in the iPad.

- Click on general.

- Then, click on software update. If you get update message.

- After updated, close all application, and restart the iPad.
