# How can I add the item to the back-order from receiving pending

**[How can I add the item to the back-order from receiving pending?]{.underline}**

- When you have not received any item from the vendor and you want to purchase that item later, you can add those items to the backorder for future Purchase orders.

- At a time to reconcile order, you can add items to backorder from receiving pending option.

<!-- -->

- **[Please follow the below steps:-]{.underline}**

<!-- -->

- Open receiving pending module.

- To open the order, click on order.

- Click on the radio button to select the item that you want to add to the back office. You can select as many items as you want.

- The backorder button will not appear in enable mode until you select any item.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.75in"}

- At the bottom, click or tap on the Backorder button.

- It will appear the message to item move to backorder successfully.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.763888888888889in"}

- To view the back order list, open generate order module.

- Click on the back order list.

- Added items will have appeared in the Backorder list.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.694444444444445in"}
