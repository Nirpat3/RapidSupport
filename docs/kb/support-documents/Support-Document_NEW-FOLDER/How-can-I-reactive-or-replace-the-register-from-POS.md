# How can I reactive or replace the register from POS

**[How can I re-activate and replace the register from POS?]{.underline}**

- Open Rapid RMS application.

- Enter your store email and password.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.0755194663167105in"}

- Tap the Sign In button.

- On the right side, it will appear all released registers list below the Released registers tab.

- Click on the name of the register you would like to reactivate.

- It will appear register details on the left side of the screen.

- Click on Replace with this register red button from the right side corner of the screen.

- It will prompt a confirmation message.

- Select the 'Yes' button to release the register.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.722222222222222in"}
