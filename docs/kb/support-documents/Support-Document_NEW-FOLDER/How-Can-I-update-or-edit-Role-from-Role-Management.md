# How Can I update or edit Role from Role Management

**[How Can I update or edit Role from Role Management?]{.underline}**

1.  Login into the Back office with your credentials.

2.  Click on the Settings option.

3.  Click on the Staff menu.

4.  Select Role Management.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9992607174103236in"}

5.  Click on the Action menu button next to the role.

6.  Click on the Edit button.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="3.0113648293963253in"}

7.  Now, check the check box next to module, sub-module, and feature to access.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.500068897637795in" height="2.8152176290463693in"}

8.  If you do not want to let users access any module, sub-module, and feature uncheck the check box next to the module.

![C:\\Users\\siya\\Downloads\\Screenshot_49.png](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="2.8582983377077866in"}

9.  Tap on the Save button.
