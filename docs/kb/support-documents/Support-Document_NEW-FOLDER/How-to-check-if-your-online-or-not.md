# How to check if your online or not

**[How to check if you are online or not]{.underline}**

- Hit the setting icon available on the iPad dashboard.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.174930008748906in"}

- From the side, panel click on wi-fi.

- Toggle button for wi-fi display ON.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.127237532808399in"}

- Turn Off Wi- fi.

- Ipad will go into offline mode and the internet would disconnect.

- From the dashboard go to the Safari browser.

- Enter any URL and hit search.

- It would not open the URL which means the iPad is in offline mode.

- In the title bar of the iPad, there is no ant symbol from the internet would display which means the iPad is in offline mode.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="3.627714348206474in"}

- Now go to setting and turn on wifi.

- In the title bar, there is a symbol for wi-fi should display that indicates the iPad is connected to the internet.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.052083333333333in"}
