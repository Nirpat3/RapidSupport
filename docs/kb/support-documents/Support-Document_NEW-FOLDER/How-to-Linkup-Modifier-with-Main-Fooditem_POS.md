# How to Linkup Modifier with Main Fooditem_POS

# **[Modifier Linkup]{.underline}**

## **How to linkup modifier with main food item in POS.**

1.  Login to RapidRMS Application with your credentials.

- Enter your "**Email OR User ID**".

- Enter your "**Password**".

- Click on "**SIGN IN**" button.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9370034995625547in"}

2.  Go into "**INVENTORY**" module.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="2.6909722222222223in"}

3.  Search your main food item in "**ITEMS"** section.

**For Example : VEGETABLE BURGER**

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="3.8881944444444443in"}

Additional information : You can use filter option also to filtering your item from right side corner (**FILTER OPTION**)

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="3.298611111111111in"}

4.  Now go into item details (Vegetable Burger) with the click on "**\>**"

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="0.8347222222222223in"}

5.  Here, Please toggen ON for "**IS FOODITEM**". It will open the **MODIFIER** tab on upper side.

![](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="2.7083333333333335in"}

Let's scroll down the item details screen then you will find the "**QSR INFORMATION"** Section.

Here you can add your "**MODIFIER GROUP"** in QSR GROUP whichever group is relevant with this food item (Vegetable Burger)

![](media/image7.png){width="6.5in" height="2.701388888888889in"}

Once you click on "**\>**" then all your Modifier Group will be display, you can select relevant group and click on "**SAVE"** button.

![](media/image8.png){width="6.5in" height="4.111111111111111in"}

![](media/image9.png){width="6.5in" height="3.7041666666666666in"}

6.  Now lets go into "**MODIFITER"** tab on upper side. Click on "**ADD**" button

![](media/image10.png){width="6.5in" height="3.6944444444444446in"}

Here you will find all modifier item list of BURGER Group. Click on "**Select All**" and **SAVE** it.

![](media/image11.png){width="6.5in" height="4.3125in"}

Again "**Select All**" and Click on **SAVE** button from Main Screen.

![](media/image12.png){width="6.5in" height="4.0in"}

7.  Now lets ringup this item on Ringup Screen and click on "**CUSTOMIZE**" option.

![](media/image13.png){width="6.5in" height="3.8993055555555554in"}

8.  Once you click on CUSTOMIZE option then you can choose your modifier for your customer order.

![](media/image14.png){width="6.5in" height="3.9881944444444444in"}
