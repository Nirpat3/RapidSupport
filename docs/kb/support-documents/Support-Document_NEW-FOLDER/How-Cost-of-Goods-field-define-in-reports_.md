# How Cost of Goods field define in reports_

**How Cost of Goods field define in the report?**

The cost of goods field is related to cost of selling items.

Cost of goods is the sum of the total cost of an item(s) that is sold.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.888888888888889in"}

Cost of goods is the cost of all rings up Items. (Department cost must count from Profit, Margin, Check cash cost must zero)

[If any item is sold and cost is not defined, the item cost value is zero.]{.mark}

[Item sold by ringing up price and no cost is defined. This type of item will have cost value of zero.]{.mark}
