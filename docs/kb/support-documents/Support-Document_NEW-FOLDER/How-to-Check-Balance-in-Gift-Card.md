# How to Check Balance in Gift Card

**[How to Check the Balance in a Gift Card?]{.underline}**

If gift card is available then the balance of that gift card can be check from cash register module.

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Open Rapid RMS application.

2.  Go to the Cash Register.

3.  At the bottom bar, there is one option named as "**MORE**" click on that option.

> Click on a "**GIFTCARD**" from all of them.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.131620734908137in"}

4.  When hit gift card then there are two options available. Please click on "**Check Balance**".

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="3.4564424759405075in"}

5.  Enter gift card number which gift card is available with Customer OR Store.

> **NOTE**: If you enter a number which is not active then it will ask you to activate this entered number card. If you hit yes then a new gift card will be added.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="3.7308694225721784in"}

6.  After entering the gift card number click on check balance.

7.  Balance amount will display.

8.  If you want to print balance receipt then it can be possible by print option.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.680555555555555in"}
