# How to add sales discount from BOF and apply it on bill_

**[How to add 'Sales Discount' from BOF and apply it on the bill?]{.underline}**

- **Sales Discount is a reduced price offered on sales of goods or customer\'s total bill.**

- **Add Sales discount from BOF**

<!-- -->

- In BOF, Sign in with your store -\> Inventory -\> Discounts.

- In Discounts Page, Click on 'Add' button.

- Now add sales discount detail in its respective field.

- Now select 'Sale Discount' option if not selected then the discount will not display in POS screen of iPad.

- Now save new sales discount.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="2.986111111111111in"}

- Now you will see, newly added discount in List.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9722222222222223in"}

- **Apply a Sales discount on the bill.**

> **Follow the below steps:**

- Open Rapid RMS app.

- Go to RCR module.

- Ring up some items in bill screen.

- Now In the bottom screen, tap on the discount icon.

- Now you will see, the discount popup with sales discount list which you have already added from BOF.

- Now either selects Bill option or item. If select bill option then the discount will apply on customer bill otherwise on selected items.

- After doing the above process tap on 'Enter' button.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.755208880139983in"}

- Now you will see, applying a discount on the customer bill.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.875in"}
