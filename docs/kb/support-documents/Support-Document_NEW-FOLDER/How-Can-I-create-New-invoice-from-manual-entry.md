# How Can I create New invoice from manual entry

**[How to create a new invoice entry from manual entry?]{.underline}**

- Open rapid RMS application.

- Open the Manual entry module from the dashboard.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.527777777777778in"}

- It will appear manual entry option.

- To create a new invoice or new manual entry, click on the new option from the manual entry.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.694444444444445in"}

- It will prompt you to receive the info window.

  ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Field**        **Definition**
  ---------------- -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Receiving Date   It allows you to select a date from the receiving date picker window. Select the date on which you have received items from the vendor.

  Vendor           It allows you to select a vendor from the vendor list. Select the vendor from the list.

  Invoice \#       It allows you to enter the invoice number.
  ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Select the date on which you want to receive the purchased item.

- Select the vendor from the list. The vendor field is not compulsory. If you receive purchased items from multiple vendors, you need not select a vendor from the list.

- Enter the Invoice number.

- Click on the Next button

- Enter your user-defined invoice number. You can enter alphabet or numeric values in invoice field.

- Click on the Next button.

![](media/image3.png){width="4.6194444444444445in" height="2.173611111111111in"}

- When you click on the Next button, it will move to the manual entry page.

- It will open the new blank manual entry screen.

- There are New Item, Search, Hold, Print, Item Info, Reconcile, and Aggregate buttons available bottom of the screen.

  ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Button**   **Definition**
  ------------ -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  New Item     It allows you to create a new item from Manual entry. When you create a new item from manual entry, it will also be added to the item master in Inventory Management.

  Search       It allows you to select an item from the item master.

  Hold         It allows you to hold the manual entry.

  Print        It allows you to review, copy, save, print, and share an entry on other social media apps

  Item Info    It allows you to review the Invoice number, Vendor, Date received, total number of Products, and total cost of the product.

  Reconcile    It allows you to reconcile or update your receiving order to Inventory.

  Aggregate    It allows you to match your purchased items with all of your inventory items.
  ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Click on the search button to add items.

- Select items from the list which you want to add in manual entry and click on add button.

- You can also add the item to the Purchase order and print the label of which selected item from the list.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.694444444444445in"}

- If you have selected a vendor at invoice creation time, it will only appear item, which belongs to the selected vendor in the list or it will appear all items from the list.

- Select the items which you want to receive.

- Click on add button.

- All added items will appear in the list.

- It will appear all item details like Image, UPC, item name, Qty OH, Qty received Price, cost, and with an extended cost.

![C:\\Users\\siya\\Downloads\\MicrosoftTeams-image (105).png](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="4.8765879265091865in"}

- Click on the item to add the received quantity.

- When you click on the item, it will open the item to edit mode to enter from the manual entry.

![C:\\Users\\siya\\Downloads\\MicrosoftTeams-image (104).png](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="4.87373031496063in"}

- Enter your received quantity in the QTY OH field. You can enter Single, Cash or Pack received quantity in the QTY OH field.

- At the bottom, click on the Save button.

![C:\\Users\\siya\\Downloads\\MicrosoftTeams-image (103).png](media/image7.png){width="6.5in" height="4.87373031496063in"}

- If you want to return the item from the vendor.

- If a return is enabled then the quantity of that item would be returned from available stock and this item quantity will display with a negative sign in the invoice.

- If you want to hold the invoice, click on the hold button from the bottom bar.

- If you want to hold invoice then click on hold. The invoice will hold and display in the recall list. An invoice will stay in recall list till it not reconcile.

- After you enter all items quantity, cost, or price and want to update store click on reconcile button from the bottom bar.

- Click on Reconcile button to complete update the stock in Inventory.

- After reconciling, item stock would update in inventory.

- The invoice entry will be moved to the History section.
