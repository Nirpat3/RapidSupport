# How Drop Amount Notification Work

**[How Drop Amount Notification Work?]{.underline}**

# **There are 2 ways for Drop Amout in POS.**

1.  Drop Amount from Profile Menu

2.  Drop Amout from Notification

NOTE : Drop Amount will be work in both mode (Online & Offline).

1.  **Drop Amout from Profile Menu**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Open Rapid RMS application in iPad.

2.  Go to Cash Register module.

3.  Left Side, Click on Prifle Menu.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="2.5051246719160103in"}

4.  In Profile Menu, Click on "**DROP**" option.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="3.393160542432196in"}

5.  Popup message will be display on screen with "Do you want to open drawer?" Click on **Yes**.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.104963910761155in"}

6.  Enter your drop amount and click on "**DROP**" button.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="3.8012992125984253in"}

7.  You will get info message "**Amont dropped successfully**".

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="4.267283464566929in"}

1.  **Drop Amout from Notification**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to BackOffice(BOS) with your user credentials.

2.  Tap menu ![](media/image6.png){width="0.20499890638670165in" height="0.20499890638670165in"} (located on the top left side).

3.  Tap **SETTINGS**.

4.  Tap **STORE**.

5.  Scroll to see the "**PAYMENT TYPE**" option. You will find your existing list of Tender.

> ![](media/image7.png){width="6.5in" height="2.6682950568678914in"}

6.  For "**CASH**" Payment, In right side click on "**ACTION**" then click on "**EDIT"** to update the Drop Amount Notification.

> ![](media/image8.png){width="6.5in" height="3.1007338145231844in"}

7.  Please select "**CASH**" in Payment Type.

Please tick "**Drop Amount**" option.

Please tick "**Force Drop Amount**" option ( if you want to do Drop Amout Forcefully by Cashier, it will not allow to cancel from notification).

Please click on "**SAVE**" button.

> ![](media/image9.png){width="6.5in" height="2.745096237970254in"}

8.  In POS, once you reach your Invoice limit \$1000 then it will popup info notification message to do the drop amount. Please click on "**Drop**".

> ![](media/image10.png){width="6.5in" height="3.768885608048994in"}

9.  Popup message will be display on screen with "Do you want to open drawer?" Click on **Yes**.

> ![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.104963910761155in"}

10. Enter your drop amount and click on "**DROP**" button.

> ![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="3.8012992125984253in"}

11. You will get info message "**Amont dropped successfully**".

> ![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="4.859296806649168in"}
