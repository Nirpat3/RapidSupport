# How to generate Sales Invoices through Gift Card Payment

**[How to generate Sales Invoices through Gift Card Payment ?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to **RapidRMS Application** with your credentials.

Enter your Email OR User ID.

Enter your Password.

Click on SIGN IN button.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="2.7769488188976377in"}

2.  Go into "**CASH REGISTER - RCR**". Ener your quick password and Click on "**ENTER**" button.

3.  Ringup item's that customer wants to buy, Now Click on "**DONE**" button and proceed for the payment on Tender Screen.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="3.3451443569553807in"}

4.  Here, you can select "**GIFT CARD**" tender to collect payment and click on "**DONE**" button. It will redirect to RMS Gift Card Screen.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="3.8244903762029745in"}

5.  In RMS Gift Card Screen, Please enter Customer "**Gift Card Number"** and Click on "**SEND**" button.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="3.84205927384077in"}

6.  Click on "**DONE**" button and finish your transaction.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="4.525629921259842in"}
