# How to Activate POSLink SDK in POS

**[How to Activate POSLink SDK in POS?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to **RapidRMS Application** with your credentials.

- Enter your Email OR User ID.

- Enter your Password.

- Click on SIGN IN button.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="3.008707349081365in"}

2.  Click on "**SETTINGS**".

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="3.313840769903762in"}

3.  Ener your quick password and Click on "**ENTER**" button.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="2.969014654418198in"}

4.  Click on "**PAX CONFIGURATION**" & Please select "**POSLink SDK**" instead of Rapid SDK.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.902179571303587in"}

5.  Click on Test Connection and SAVE it.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="4.351570428696413in"}

**[NOTE]{.underline} : All other setting remain same for PAX device. Here we are using only different SDK in POS side.**
