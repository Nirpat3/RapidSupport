# How can I release the register from POS

**[How can I release the register from POS?]{.underline}**

- Click on the settings option ![C:\\Users\\mit shah\\AppData\\Local\\Microsoft\\Windows\\INetCache\\IE\\BU8Y79T3\\WMF-Agora-Settings_BCBCBC.svg\[1\].png](media/image1.png){width="0.17463910761154855in" height="0.17463910761154855in"} available at the bottom of the DashBoard.

![C:\\Users\\mit shah\\Downloads\\MicrosoftTeams-image (8).png](media/image2.jpeg){width="6.268055555555556in" height="3.65541447944007in"}

- Enter your quick access password.

- Click on the Module Set up option from the left side list.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.249408355205599in"}

- As per company norms, rules and regulations, you need to log in again.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.0755194663167105in"}

- Enter your store credential. Enter your Email address and password.

- Click on Sign in button.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="4.666666666666667in"}

- It will appear Activation page.

- On the right side, click on the Active register tab.

- It will appear the list of active registers below the Active register tab.

- Your active register name will appear in the active register tab as selected by default.

- By default, the active register name will be mentioned in blue color in the active registers tab.

- On the left side, you will see the register details.

- To release the register, click on 'Release this register' option.

![](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="4.694444444444445in"}

- It will prompt a confirmation message.

- Select the 'Yes' button to release the register.
