# How to download app from app store

**How to download the app from the app store?**

- Start IPad.

- Hit the app store icon from the dashboard.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.3063867016622925in"}

- Click on the search button and enter the text Rapid RMS.

- Click on the download option which is available beside the application name.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.363920603674541in"}

- When you click on the download button it will appear downloading process on the dashboard.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="3.979886264216973in"}

- It will take a few minutes to download the app.

- After completion of the download app will display on an iPad dashboard.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="3.7767563429571305in"}
