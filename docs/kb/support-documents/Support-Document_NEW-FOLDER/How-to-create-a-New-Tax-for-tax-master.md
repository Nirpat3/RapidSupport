# How to create a New Tax for tax master

**[How to create a New Tax for tax master?]{.underline}**

Tax master is a module in which you can create a new tax, Edit an existing tax, and delete tax.

- Open RAPID RMS application in IOS.

- Select inventory management from Dashboard.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="3.3834022309711287in"}

- Click on three line option from top left side corner of the screen.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="3.738610017497813in"}

- It will appear the list of existing taxes.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.047619203849519in"}

- Click on Add button from the bottom of the screen.

+-----------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Field**             | **Description**                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
+:=====================:+===============================================================================================================================================================================================================================+
| Tax                   | It describes the **name** of the tax.                                                                                                                                                                                         |
+-----------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Type                  | It describes the **type** of the tax.                                                                                                                                                                                         |
+-----------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Amount                | It describes the total **amount** of tax.                                                                                                                                                                                     |
+-----------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Tax Included in Price | 1.  When you turn on the **tax included in price** button, the tax amount includes in the retail price of an item. It is called an **Inclusive Tax**.                                                                         |
|                       |                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
|                       | 2.  When you do not turn on the Tax included in rice button, the tax amount does not include in the price of an item. The tax amount is separately charged on the retail price of an item. It is called an **Exclusive Tax**. |
+-----------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

- Enter your Tax name.

- Select Percentage type.

- Enter percentage amount.

**Note:-** If you want to include tax amount in retail price of an item. Turn on Tax included in price button.

Turn on Tax Included in Price button.

- Tap on the Save button.
