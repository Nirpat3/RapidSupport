# How can we activate GIFT CARD Service from BackOffice

**[How can we activate GIFT CARD Service from BackOffice ?]{.underline}**

- **Please follow the below steps:-**

1.  Login to BackOffice with your credentials.

2.  Tap menu ![hamburger-menu-icon-png-white-24.jpg](media/image1.png){width="0.2075404636920385in" height="0.2075404636920385in"} (located on the top left side)

3.  Click on "**INVENTORY**" Section.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="2.484831583552056in"}

4.  In INVENTORY, click on "**DEPARTMENT**" option.

In Department, You will find "Departmet Setting" option.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="1.2338987314085739in"}

5.  In Department Setting, You can ON Gift Card Services.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="3.274475065616798in"}
