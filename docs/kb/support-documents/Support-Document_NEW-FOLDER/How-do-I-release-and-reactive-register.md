# How do I release and reactive register

**How do I release and reactive register?**

> **Release a register**

- On the dashboard, tap on the setting icon at the bottom of the screen.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="4.527777777777778in"}

- A left-hand sidebar will appear, click on module setup.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="4.805555555555555in"}

- For security reasons, you will need to log in again.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="4.666666666666667in"}

- You will now see the module activation screen. On the right, click on Active Register. Then, select which register you would like to release.

- On the left, you will see the register details. Click the red button to release this register.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="4.694444444444445in"}

- It will ask you to confirm, and then your register will be released.

> **Reactive or replace a register**

- Open the app and enter your username and password.

- Select the store for this device.

- You will now see the module activation screen. On the right, click on released register. Click on the name of the register you would like to reactivate.

- On the left, you will see the register details. Click the red button to replace with this register.

- It will ask you to confirm and then your register will be replaced.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="4.722222222222222in"}
