# ALTRIA_PMUSA ScanData File Submission Process

Version: 1.0

# **[ALTRIA FILE SUBMISSION PROCESS]{.underline}**

- Once you finish registration process with ALTRIA then you will receive welcome email from ALTRIA along with TEST FILE request. Please refer below screensort.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="3.28125in"}

- You will receive Two additional email from ALTRIA to submitting your Scan Data file. These email contain details about FTP Credential (One email for Username & one email for Password).

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="3.125in"}

**[NOTE]{.underline}** : Please share these two email with Rapid RMS team on <support@rapidrms.com>.

- Once Rapid RMS Team received your FTP emails then we will setup your ALTRIA account in Rapid RMS Back-Office.

- Once ALTRIA account setup is done then we will intimate you to submit your ALTRIA TEST FILE.

Please follow the below steps and screenshot to submit your ALTRIA TEST FILE.

1.  Login to BackOffice with your credentials.

2.  Tap menu ![hamburger-menu-icon-png-white-24.jpg](media/image3.png){width="0.2075404636920385in" height="0.2075404636920385in"} (located on the top left side).

3.  Click on "**SCAN DATA**" Module.

4.  Click on "**ALTRIA FILE**" option.

5.  Select Date Period.

6.  Click on **"Upload 2022 File"** Tab.

7.  Click on "**Upload**" button.

![](media/image4.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9900831146106737in"}

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="1.9270833333333333in"}

- You can check your file submitted status in FILE Section.

![](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9674825021872264in"}
