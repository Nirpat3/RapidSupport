# How to get invoice according to time duration

**How to get an invoice according to time duration?**

> This functionality is used to check all invoices according to time duration.

- In Dashboard, Select Cash Register module and enter 4 digits quick access.

- In RCR screen, Click on the top left menu.

- Click on invoice option.

![](media/image1.png){width="6.5in" height="3.1875in"}

- In Calendar, click on the Today button.

- After clicking on the Today button, It will display a pop up with different time range. I.e. today, yesterday, this week, last week, this month, last month etc.

- Let suppose you want to see last month invoices then apply option 'last month'.

- All invoices of last month will display.
