# Filter items list to select items from third party promotions for RJR scan Data

**[How to filter items list to select items from third party promotions for RJR scan Data?]{.underline}**

1.  Login to BOS with your user credentials.

2.  In the top right, click on three line hamburger ![hamburger-menu-icon-png-white-24.jpg](media/image1.png){width="0.2075404636920385in" height="0.2075404636920385in"} menu.

3.  Choose Third party promotion option from settings.

![](media/image2.png){width="6.5in" height="3.0108377077865267in"}

4.  Select the existing third party discount from the list.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.5in" height="3.5976793525809274in"}

5.  Click on ![C:\\Users\\siya\\AppData\\Local\\Microsoft\\Windows\\INetCache\\IE\\EHUWMOOS\\plus_PNG121\[1\].png](media/image4.png){width="0.21738517060367454in" height="0.21738517060367454in"} plus button is located beside the item name.

6.  Click on the action button and select the Edit option from the drop down list.

![](media/image5.png){width="6.5in" height="2.994536307961505in"}

7.  Click on the Next button.

8.  At the top left side, click on the filter button.

![](media/image6.png){width="6.5in" height="3.0240693350831145in"}

9.  You can filter items Department wise, Vendor wise, and Group wise.

![](media/image7.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9809525371828522in"}\
\
![](media/image8.png){width="6.5in" height="2.971719160104987in"}

10. Now, select any option of the filter.

![](media/image9.png){width="6.5in" height="2.9866404199475065in"}\
**Note:-** Selected filter option will appear at the top left side corner of the screen.

11. You can also select the multiple filter options.

12. At bottom, click on the Apply button.

![](media/image10.png){width="6.5in" height="2.981560586176728in"}

13. Click on ![](media/image11.png){width="0.29438429571303587in" height="0.29347878390201226in"} the cancel button.

14. When you select any filter option, it will review items as per the selected filter option.

![](media/image12.png){width="6.5in" height="3.0814096675415574in"}

15. Now, Click on the Next button.

16. At the top right side, Tap on Finish.
